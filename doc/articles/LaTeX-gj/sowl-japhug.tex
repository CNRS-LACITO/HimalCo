        \begin{frame} 
      \frametitle{Pronouns}
      The verb \ipa{sɯso} `think' is transitive, so the absence of ergative marking on \ipa{ɯʑo} `he' shows that this pronoun is the S of the complement clause, not the A of the main verb.
      
\begin{exe}
