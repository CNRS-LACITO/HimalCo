Ŋ - ŋ

ŋak   vt. reduce, break in half. 1s: ŋʌgu. 1d: ŋʌki. sʌ̄ŋpo ʔuʦōŋ bloʣhūŋʔɛ ŋâːktɛ. The storm broke off the top of the tree. ʔuŋʌ gagribîm ku ŋʌgutʌ. I emptied half the water pitcher.

ŋi   vt. hear. ʔʌ̄mʔɛ ɦoɔ̄i ŋitɛ. He heard the noise. ʔênʔɛ lêl ʔiŋūːtnu. You heard the song.

ŋin   vi. be afraid. 1s: ŋʌ̄iŋʌ. 1d: ŋīːʦi, ŋʉ̄ːʦi. thūːnɛmbi lɛmthiŋʌtʌlo moskʌ ŋīːstʌ. Walking in the forest, I was afraid of bears. ʔûŋ kʌwʌbi kʉppɵkʌ ŋīːstʌ. I was afraid of the ghost in the river.

ŋint   vt. fear. 1px: ŋʌ̄ndu. 1d: ŋīːʦi. thūːnɛmbi lɛmthiŋʌtʌlo mos ŋʌ̂ntʌ. When I walked in the forest, I feared the bears.

ŋʉr   vi. roar. 1px: ŋʌ̄r. nɵ̂r ŋʉ̂rtɛ. The tiger roared. bhʌ̂l jāːtɛlo kʌwʌ ŋʉ̂rtɛ. The river roared when it flooded. bloʣhuŋ ŋʉ̂rtɛ. The storm roared.

ŋʉrt   vt. roar at. nɵ̂rʔɛ ʔûŋ ʔiŋʉrʌtʌ. The tiger roared at me. khlêːpʔɛ ɦʌs ʔiŋʌ̄rki. Dogs bark at people. nɵ̂rʔɛ ghrot ŋʌ̄rdʉ. The tiger roars at the goat.

ŋet   vi. be sick, hurt. 1s: ŋêiŋʌ. 1d: ŋeʦi. ʔʌdhōŋ ŋestɛ. I have a headache.

ŋent   vt. desire, want, long for. 1s: ŋēndu. 1d: ŋēːʦi. ʦɵʦʦɵʔɛ ʦʌkleʈ ŋêntɛ. The child longed for chocolate. bîrmɵʔɛ ghiu ŋêndetʉ. The cat longs for ghee.

ŋɛn   vi. sit. 1s: ŋɛ̄iŋʌ. 1d: ŋɛ̄ːʦi. ʔûŋ kurʦibi ŋɛ̄ːstʌ. I sat on the chair.

ŋɛnt   vt. put on. ʔuŋʌ ʔʌ̄m kurʦibi ŋɛ̄ndu. I make him sit on the chair. ʔʌ̄mʔɛ ʦʌutarabi kʉ̂r ŋɛ̂ntʉtɛ. I put the load on the resting platform. kʌrmiʔɛ kʌ̄m mʉtɛlo lɛ̂l kʌ̄mpo laskʌ ŋɛ̂ntɛ. When the mason was building the house, he first installed the door.

ŋɛmt   vt-i. turn blue from choking (cyanosis); have a hangdog look (after having been scolded). 3s: ŋɛ̂mkhʌtɛ. 1s: ʔiŋɛmʌŋʌ. 1d: ʔiŋɛ̂mkhɵʦi. 3s: ŋɛ̄mdʉ. simbʌlki ʦɵʦʦɵpo ʔughoɔ̄ndʉrʉbi ɦɛptɛlo ŋɛ̂mkhʌtɛ. The yam got stuck in the child's throat and he turned blue. ʔupɛpʔɛ bhʌ̂rtɛlo ʔuʦɵ ŋɛ̂mkhʌtɛ. After his father scolded him, the child had a hangdog look.

ŋɛl   vi. play a trick on someone, lie. 1px: ŋɛ̄lŋʌ. ʔʌ̄m ŋɛ̂ltɛnʌ koɔ̄ibi rʌ̄m dhuma ghole bektɛ. He played a trick and put too much salt in the curry. dēlkʌmbi turetureŋâm ʦɵʦʦɵɦɛm mununʌkho mʌri ŋɛ̄lnu. If there many like-minded children in the village, they will be naughty. ʔuŋʌ ŋɛ̄lnɛ mujɛdu. I don't like to be naughty. ʔûː ʣhōŋkilo ʔubhɵ̂l gɵpɛ goru mʌri ŋɛ̄l. A strong ox does not pull straight when we plough fields. REVISE.

ŋɛl   vt. not be properly cooked. 3sn: ŋɛ̄ːlʉ. khos khʌpkilo miʔɛ muʦɛ̄ːbʉkho ŋɛ̄ːlʉ. If the fire is not strong enough, it does not cook well when we cook yam.

ŋɵk   vi. cry. 1s: ŋɵkʌtʌ. 1d: ŋɵkiti. ghole ŋôːnɛ miroɔmpo lagi bʌ̄ŋpɛ muʦhûː. A lot of crying is not good for the health.

ŋɵl   vi. be mixed up.

vt. mix up. 1s: ŋolu. 1d: ŋɵli. ʔis-saman ŋɵ̂ltɛ. Our things got mixed up. ʔʌ̄msupo ʔus-bheɖa ŋɵ̂ltɛnu. Their sheep got mixed up. sɵ̂rkolo dhōːʦer ŋɵ̂ltɛ. The rice and the wheat got mixed. kukolo dʉt ŋoɔ̄l. Milk dilutes in water.

ŋɵl-si   vr. come together (to a wedding ceremony). bijabi khoɔ̂nnɛ ŋoɔ̄lsinɛ mʌttʉ. We need to come together to go to this marriage ceremony.
