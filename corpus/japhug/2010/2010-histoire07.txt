paχɕi kɯphɯt ci ɣɤʑu
ɯmbur ra torku, kuxtɕo ɯŋgɯ ra tarku
tshɤt ɯkɯmtshi ci thɯɣe
nɯnɯ cho ci kɤnɯrŋɤrɯrundʑi tɕe
tshɤt ɯkɯmtshi nɯ lanɯri
kɯmaqhu tɕe, kɯmaʁ ci thɯɣe, nɯ kɯ zixingche ɲɤznɯmbrɤpɯ
si ɯqa ri thazɣɯt tɕe, nɯreri pɯrɤʑi
zixingche ra ɯthoʁ ri paɣɤntaβ
ɯndɤcu si taʁ rɟɤskɤt tothɯ tɕe, nɯ ɯtaʁ ri tari
paχɕi ɯkɯphɯt tari tɕe,
nɯ jamar tɕe, kɯmaʁ ci thɯɣe tɕe,
zixingche ɯtaʁ ri kuxtɕo ɯŋgɯ, pingguo torku.
tɕe torku tɕe thaɣɯt tɕendɤre
ɯʁɤri ri tɕe kɯmaʁ ci kɯnɤmbrɤpɯ ci kɤɣe
tamɯrpundʑi, tɕe ɯʑo pɯndʐaβ tɕe
ɯpaχɕi ra choʁndɤr
ci nɯ jonɯɕe
kɯmaʁ χsɯm jaɣenɯ tɕe, nɯnɯ 
paχɕi pɯkɯʁndɤr nɯ ra tawumnɯ tarkunɯ, zixingche ra tazrɤrunɯ
tɕendere, kɯmaqhu tɕe, 
janɯri ri, ɯrte ɲɯnɯβde
tɯrdoʁ nɯ kɯ ɯrte pjɤmto tɕe, 
tɤfsit thalɤt tɕendɤre ɯrte nɯ ʑnakho
