kutɕu tʂu pɯ-kɤ-ɣe, pɯ-ɣe-a nɯ tu-ti-a ɕi ?
aʑo kɯre nɤkinɯ "pɯ-ɣi" ɲɯ-tɯ-ti tɕendɤre @chepiao ɕ-tɤ-χtɯ-t-a, @chepiao ɕ-tɤ-χtɯ-t-a tɕe tɕendɤre, krɤɕi @qiche ɯ-taʁ pɯ-ɣe-a.
jɯfɕɯndʐɯsŋi pɯ-ɣe-a, tɕe jɯfɕɯndʐɯsŋi pɯ-ɣe-a ri, tɕe @jinchuan nɯ-azɣɯt-i ri, nɤkinɯ tɕe, "tʂu pjɤ-mbɯt" ɲɯ-ti-nɯ ri.
tɕendɤre "tʂu pjɤ-mbɯt" ɲɯ-ti tɕe, dala thɯ-ɣe-j tɕe, daltsɯtsa thɯ-ɣe-j tɕe, tɕendɤre, nɤkinɯ,
tɕɤndi @ganzi tʂu pɕoʁ nɯ-ɣe-j ma nɤkinɯ @kangding ɯ-pɕoʁ tɤ-ɣe-j 
tɕeri @kangding tʂu pjɤ-mbɯt, tɕe tʂu pjɤ-mbɯt tɕe ɯ-kɯ-βzu maŋe.
tɕendɤre ɯ-kɯ-βzu maŋe qhendɤre, nɯre khro pɯ-nɯna-j, pɯ-rɤʑi-j.
kɯ-maqhu tɕendɤre @jiashiyuan, @abazhou ɣɯ @jiashiyuan ra kɯ ɯ-jɯ nɤkinɯ @zhuangzaiji ci ɣɯ ɯ-@you chu-orɕo qhendɤre, 
nɯ ɯ-@you nɯ pa-lɤt-nɯ, ʑara nɯ-@qiche ŋgɯ pa-tɕɤt-nɯ. tɕendɤre, @qiche ɯ-ŋgɯ pa-tɕɤt-nɯ qhendɤre, @zhuangzaiji ɣɯ ɯ-@you pa-lɤt-nɯ.
tɕendɤre, tha-βde tɕendɤre, tʂu pɯ-kɯ-mbɯt ʁnɯ-khɤl ɣɤʑu tɕe, kɤfsɯfse tha-βde.
tɕendɤre kɯmaqhu tɕe ji-tʂu tɤɲɟɯ tɕe tɕendɤre, nɯ-ɣe-j tɕendɤre @kangding thɯ-ari-j.
@kangding thɯ-azɣɯt-i ri tɕendɤ nɤkinɯ nɯ jamar tɕe, nɤkinɯ "tianquan 天全 tʂu pjɤ-mbɯt tɕe sɤxɕe maŋe" ɲɯ-ti.
tɕendɤre nɤkinɯ @luding 泸定 ndɤre tɕe, @guanzi ŋgɯ ri ɕ-tɤ-rɯndzɤtshi-j. ɕ-tɤ-rɯndzɤtshi-j tɕe ɯ-pa nɤkinɯ @lvguan ŋgɯ ɕ-pɯ-nɯ-rma-j.
@lvguan ŋgɯ ɕpɯnɯrmaj tɕe ɯfsosoz tɤrɤruj tɕe kɤnɯtʂhaj. kɤ-nɯtʂha-j ndɤre, tɕe li "sɤxɕe maŋe" ɲɯ-ti, @dianhua li jɤ-lɤt-i ri "sɤxɕe maŋe" ɲɯ-ti.
tɕe saχsɯ ɕɯŋgɯ pɯ-nɯ-rɤʑi-j, tʂha kɤ-tshi-j. tɕeri, nɯ jamar tɕe, "pjɯ-kɯ-ɕe ŋu" ɲɯ-ti-nɯ ri, nɤkinɯ @tianquan mɤɕtʂa pɯ-ɣe-j, tɕe tɤ-nɯsaχsɯ-j, @guanzi ŋgɯ ri.
tɕendɤre tɤ-nɯsaχsɯ-j tɕe nɤkinɯ @tianquan mɤɕtʂa, ɯ-locu mɤɕtʂa thɯ-ɣe-j.
@tianquan mɤɕtʂa thɯ-ɣe-j ri, rca nɯ, ɯ-rca kɯ-tu maŋe, tʂu ɯ-tɯ-mbɯt ɯ-grɤl maŋe tɕendɤre 
nɯre @tianquan ɣɯ @modi kɯfse, nɤkinɯ @motuo ɯ-kɯ-ndo ra kɯ, nɯra "maka @chengdu tɯ-ɕe-nɯ pjɯ-ta-tsɯm-nɯ maʁ thi, pɯ-kɯ-mbɯt mɤɕtʂa ɣɯ chɯ-ta-lɤt-nɯ" ntsɯ ɲɯ-ti. 
iʑora ŋgɯ pɯ-ɕti-j tɕe, "mɤra" tɤ-tɯt-i, tɕe thɯ-ɣe-j tɕendɤre tɕethi, @tianquan mɤɕtʂa thɯ-ɣe-j ri, kɤ-qanɯ, tɕe kɤ-qanɯ tɕendɤre, nɯreri pɯ-rɤʑi-j.
kɯ-maqhu tɕendɤre, ɯ-locu cha @pijiu ɕ-ta-χtɯ-nɯ, kɤndza tsuku ɕ-ta-χtɯ-nɯ tɕendɤre, tɕethi tʂu, tʂɤχcɤl ri pɯ-rɤgrɤl-i, 
tɕendɤre nɯreri @pijiu ra kɤ-tshi-j, tɕendɤre nɯ kɯ-fse nɯ pɯnɯrɤʑij tɕe, @keche ŋgɯ pɯ-rŋgɯ-j, @keche ŋgɯ pɯ-rŋgɯ-j tɕe,
tɕendɤre kɯ-maqhu tɕe, nɤkinɯ, li tʂu ɕɤr tu-βzu-nɯ ɲɯ-ŋu ɲɯ-ti-nɯ tɕe, kɤ-nɤmdzɯ-j, pɯ-nɤmdzɯ-j tɕendɤre,
kɯmaqhu tɕe, @sandian ko-zɣɯt tɕendɤre, tʂu to-ɲɟɯ ɲɯ-ti-nɯ. tɕendɤre tʂu to-ɲɟɯ ɲɯ-ti-nɯ tɕendɤre, nɯ thɯ-ɣe-j.
tɕendɤre thɯ-ɣe-j ri tʂu kɤ-βzu ɲɤ-sthɯt-nɯ. tɕe kɤ-βzu ɲɤ-sthɯt-nɯ tɕe, tɕe ʁo kɤ-nɯɬoʁ-i kɤ-nɯɬoʁ-i tɕendɤre thɯ-ɣe-j tɕe,
rcanɯ, maka @qiche nɯ wuma sɯrɟɯɣ tha-βzu tha-sɯrɟɯɣ nɤ tha-sɯrɟɯɣ. kɯ-maqhu tɕe @jiashiyuan ɯ-ʑɯβ ɲɯ-ɣi, tɯtshot ɣnɤsqɯ-skɤrma jamar pɯ-nɯʑɯβ.
tɕendɤre tʂu ɯ-rkɯ ri ka-ndo tɕe, tɕendɤre aʑɯβ ra hanɯni ɲɤ-ʑi jamar tɕe li thɯ-ɣe-j tɕe, (400)

nɯre tɕe, @chuzu nɯ-ɕar-a, tɕendɤre nɯreri tɕe tɕe, @chuzu ɯ-ŋgɯ thɯ-ɣe-a tɕe, tɕe aki kɯreri, tha chɯ-tɯ-zɣɯt tɕe, nɤ-@dianhua thɯ-lat-a ŋu.
tɕe nɤ-@dianhua tɤ-lat-a tɕe, nɤj pɯ-tɯ-mtshɤm tɕe, nɯ koʁmɯz thɯ-azɣɯt-a ŋu !
