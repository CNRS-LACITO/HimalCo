tɤzrɤj nɯ si ci ŋu, mɤ-mbro.
tɕe ɯ-ru nɯ tɕhi ɲɯ-jpum, tɯ-jaʁmu ma ɲɯ-jpum mɤ-cha.
tɕeri ɯnɯnɯ si nɯnɯ, ɯ-ru nɯ wuma ʑo mpɕɤr.
tɕe kɯɕɯŋgɯ tɕe mbrɯtɕɯ ɯ-ŋgɯ chɯ-kɤ-rku ndʑu kɯ-fse ɲɯ-nɯ-βzu-nɯ pɯ-ŋgrɤl.
tɕe pasrɯ ɯ-mdoʁ tsa ɲɯ-ɤsɯ-ndo tɕe mpɕɤr tɕe,
nɯ kɯ-fse ɲɯ-nɯ-βzu-nɯ ŋgrɤl tɕe nɯnɯ tɤzrɤj rmi.
tɕe nɯnɯ si mɤ-kɯ-mbro ci ɕti.
tɕendɤre li qaɕparaz kɤ-ti ci tu tɕe,
qaɕparaz nɯ sɯjno, ɯ-jwaʁ kɯ-tu ŋu.
tɕe tɯ-xpa tɯ-xpa tu-ɬoʁ qhe tɕe qartsɯ tɕe pjɯ-khrɯ ɕti.
ma nɯnɯ qaɕparaz ŋu tɕe, nɯnɯ mɤ-sɤndɤɣ, fsapaʁ ra kɯ ndza-nɯ.
tɯrme ɯ-kɯ-ndza ra me ri, fsapaʁ ra kɯ tu-ndza-nɯ ŋu.
tɕe nɯ qaɕparaz ŋu. 
tɕe ɯ-jwaʁ nɯra cɤɕna ɯ-jwaʁ tsa fse.
tɕe ɯ-ku tɕu lu-omtɕoʁ tsa ŋu,
tɕe ɯ-thɤcu nɯra kɯ-xtɕɯ-xtɕi kɯ-ɤrɤrkhɯrkhe tu.
tɕendɤre khro mɤ-mpɕu, rʁom tsa rtshartsha
tɕeri, fsapaʁ ra kɯ tu-ndza-nɯ ɕti.
tɕe qaɕpa rasti kɤ-ti ŋu ɕi, qaɕpa ɣɯ ɯ-raz ŋu nɯ mɤxsi.
