iʑora tɕe nɤki, rɟitpa nɯ pɯ́-wɣ-fɕɤt tɕe,
tɯ-mu kɯ, tɯ-mu pɕoʁ nɯ tɤ́-wɣ-rtsɯz tɕe, tɯ-mu pɕoʁ rɟitpa tu-kɯ-rtsi ŋu,
tɯ-wa pɕoʁ pa-rtsɯz-nɯ tɕe, li tɯ-wa pɕoʁ rɟitpa nɯ pjɯ-rtsi-nɯ ɕti tɕe, 
tɕe iʑɤɣ tɕe, aʑo nɯ praʁwɯ rɟitpa ŋu-a tɕe, 
a-rɟit ni praʁwɯ rɟitpa kɯnɤ kɤ-rtsi ŋu-ndʑi,
χpɤltɕin nɯnɯ taʁrdo rɟitpa ŋu tɕe,
χpɤltɕin nɯ aʑo a-χti ŋu ko, a-nmaʁ.
tɕe nɯnɯ ɣɯ tɤ-rɟit nɯni li taʁrdo rɟitpa kɯnɤ kɤ-rtsi ŋu-ndʑi,
tɕe ʁnaʁna kɤ-rtsi ɕti.
ma ʑakastaka me.
