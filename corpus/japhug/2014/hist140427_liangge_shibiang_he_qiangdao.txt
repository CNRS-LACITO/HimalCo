ʁmaʁmi ʁnɯz chondɤre, nɤkinɯ, kɯ-mɯrkɯ (nɯ)
kɯ-mɯrkɯ nɯ, nɤki, tɕhi tu-ti-a pɯ-ŋu?
tɕaχpa, nɯnɯ tɕaχpa.
ʁmaʁmi ʁnɯz pjɤ-tu-ndʑi tɕe, tɯtɯrca tu-ŋke-ndʑi pjɤ-ŋu tɕe,
tʂu jɤ-ari-ndʑi tɕe, nɤkinɯ 
nɤki, iɕqha, tɕaχpa ci pjɤ-tu tɕe, 
tɕe ndʑi-laχtɕha nɯra ɣɯ-nɯsɯkho-ndʑi pjɤ-ŋu.
tɯ-rdoʁ nɯ rcanɯ, nɯ ɕɯmɯma ʑo jo-phɣo tɕe ko-kɤnbaʁ-ci.
kɯmaʁ nɯ pɯ-pɯ-ŋu nɤ,
maka ɯ-sni ra /to/ to-ɣɤwxti ʑo tɕe jo-ɕe tɕe,
iɕqha tɕaχpa nɯ cho to-kɤlɯlɤt-ndʑi tɕe,
tɕaχpa nɯ pjɤ-sat
tɕendɤre nɯra pa-sat ɯ-qhu tɕe tɕendɤre kɯmaʁ kɤ-kɯ-ɤnbaʁ nɯ jo-nɯ-ɬoʁ.
tɕendɤre nɤki, jo-rɟɯɣ ʑo jo-nɯ-ɬoʁ tɕe,
scapa ra lo-tɕɤt, tɕendɤre ɯ-stɤnga ra ɲɤ-tɕɤt tɕe chɤ-βde
scapa ra lo-tɕɤt tɕe ɯ-xɕɤt kɯ-kɯ-tu ʑo to-ti,
"/aʑo/ aʑo nɯʁɤri-a jɤɣ" to-ti ɲɯ-ŋu
tɕendɤre "aʑo ku-sɯxtsam-a ra ma, (complementation strategy)
kɯki tɯrme, nɤkinɯ, tɯrme na-nɯsɯkho pa-nɯtɕaχpa nɯ,
tɕhi kɯ-fse ci ɲɯ-ŋu-a, ku-sɯxtsam-a ra" to-ti ɲɯ-ŋu. (125 relative)
tɕendɤre iɕqha, ɯ-sni kɯ-wxti nɯnɯ kɯ, nɤki ʁmaʁmi nɯ kɯ to-ti,
"aʑo nɤʑo kɯ iɕqha nɯ tɕu ɣɯ-tu-kɯ-qur-a a-pɯ-ŋu tɕe, 
nɯnɯ wuma ʑo pɯ-nɤpe-t-a ma, (140 counterfactual)
tɯrju tsuku ma mɯ-tɤ-tɯ-nɯ-tɯt kɯnɤ pɯ-pe ma (140)
tham, tɕendɤre aʑo ta-nɤstu tɕe nɯnɯ tɤ-tɯ-tɯt nɯra nɤ-stu ŋu nɯ, ta-nɤstu ma (150)
