qamɯrwa cho xɕiri
qamɯrwa nɯnɯ, nɤʀi, ɯ-thoʁ pjɤ-kɤtɤr-ci tɕe ɯ-thoʁ pjɤ-zɣɯt.
tɕeri xɕiri kɯ pjɤ-mto tɕe to-nɤsŋɯt.
tɕeri qamɯrwa nɯ kɯ "wortɕhi ʑo, ɲɯ-kɯ-ta-a" to-ti.
tɕeri xɕiri nɯ kɯ "maka mɤ-ta-ta ma nɤki, maka mɤ-ta-lɤt ma aʑo rcanɯ sɤ-xtɕɯ-xtɕi jɤznɤ, nɤkinɯ,
pɣa kɯ-fse tu-qhe-a me tɕe, mɤ-ta-ta" to-ti ɲɯ-ŋu. (superlative)
tɕeri qamɯrwa kɯ "aʑo βʑɯ ɕti-a ma, nɤki, pɣa maʁ-a" to-ti ɲɯ-ŋu.
tɕendɤre, ɲɤ́-wɣ-lɤt.
tɕe nɯ ɯ-qhu tɕe, tɕendɤre qamɯrwa nɯ li pjɤ-kɤtɤr-ci tɕe, ɯ-thoʁ pjɤ-zɣɯt tɕe,
tɕendɤre, kɯmaʁ xɕiri kɯ /to/ tó-wɣ-nɤsŋɯt tɕe /to/ kó-wɣ-ndo.
tɕendɤre, "wojɤr nɤ wojɤr, ma-tɤ-kɯ-ndza-a" ntsɯ to-ti ɲɯ-ŋu.
tɕeri xɕiri nɯ kɯ, "aʑo ta-ndza ɕti ma,
maka βʑɯ kɯ-fse tu-qhe-a me" to-ti.
tɕeri qamɯrwa nɯ kɯ, nɤki to-ti ɲɯ-ŋu.
"aʑo, nɤki, pɣa ɕti-a ma βʑɯ maʁ-a " to-ti ɲɯ-ŋu.
tɕe li ɲɤ́-wɣ-lɤt.
tɕendɤre, qamɯrwa nɯ kɯ ɯ-rmi ʁnɯ-ɣjɤn ɲɤ-nɤsci tɕe,
tɕendɤre, nɤkinɯ, kɤ-si mɤ-kɯ-ra to-nɯ-βzu.
tɕe kɯki χpi nɯ pɯ-pɯ-ŋu nɤ,
tɯʑo, nɤki, nɯ kɯ-fse mɤ-kɯ-nɯɣɯjpa nɯ-kɯ-ɤtɯɣ tɕe,
tɕhi kɤ-cha ʑo, tshi tsuku ɲɯ-kɤ-ʑɣɤ-βzɟɯr ftɕaka tú-wɣ-βzu tɕe, nɤkinɯ
kɯ-sɤɣʑɯr mɤ-kɯ-ra phɤn" tu-ti ɲɯ-ŋu.
