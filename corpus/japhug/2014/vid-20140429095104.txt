kɯki sɤndɤr. kɯki sɤndɤr.
iʑora kɯrɯ ra tɕe sɤndɤr tu-kɯ-ti ŋu.
tɕendɤre, nɯ fse khɤftsɯɣ chɯ-kɤ-lɤt nɯnɯ tu-ta-sɯxɕɤt tɕe,
nɯ fse khɤftsɯɣ chɯ-kɤ-lɤt nɯnɯ, kɯki nɯ li kɤ-tʂɯβ thamtɕɤt nɯnɯ, raz nɯ ʁnɯz nɯ ci ntsɯ tú-wɣ-sɯpa ra tɕe,
tɕe nɤki, nɯ fse ʑo chɯ-kɤ-tʂɯβ nɯnɯ tɕe, 
ki ʁnɯz kɯni ci tú-wɣ-sɯpa tɕe, ki chɯ́-wɣ-stu,
iʑora tɕe ki chɯ-stu-j ɕti wo kɤ-rɤtʂɯβ,
kupa ra tɕe ki tu-stu-nɯ ŋu, taqaβ ɯ-ndo ki tu-stu-nɯ. ( 043)
iʑora tɕe ki chɯ-stu-j ŋu tɕe ki tú-wɣ-stu tɕe,
tɕe ɯ-pa nɯ tú-wɣ-sɯmɟa kɤ-ti nɯ, nɯnɯ ŋu. 
ki tú-wɣ-stu tɕe,
tɕe taʁ nɯ ri pjɯ́-wɣ-tʂɯβ, pa nɯ ri tú-wɣ-sɯmɟa tɕe kɯki,
tɕe ki tú-wɣ-stu ŋu.
tɕe nɯ fse chɯ-kɤ-tʂɯβ nɯ ki tú-wɣ-stu ŋu.
