kɯɕɯŋgɯ tɕe, nɤki, xiyin kɯ-rmi ci pjɤ-tu, musha kɯ-rmi ci pjɤ-tu tɕe,
tɕendɤre tɯtɯrca ʑo /jɤ/ to-k-ɤɣɯzda-ndʑi tɕe, aiji ra ɣɯ nɯ-shoudu kailuo kɯ-rmi nɯtɕu ju-ɕe-ndʑi pjɤ-ŋu.
tɕeri ʁnaʁna ʑo pjɤ-ŋgɯ-ndʑi, 
tɕe yixian?? kɤ-ti nɯ wuma ʑo, nɤkinɯ, kɯ-frtɤn ci tɯrme ɯ-stu kɯ-nɤma ci pjɤ-ŋu, (020)
tɯrme ra kɤ-qur ra wuma ʑo pjɤ-rga,
tɕendɤre tɯrme ra kɯ iɕqha tɯrme kɯ-pe nɯ bolaomu to-sɤrmi-nɯ.
tɕendɤre musha nɯnɯ tɕendɤre pjɤ-rɯpjɤβlaʁ, pjɤ-rɯkhramba qhe, 
tshɯrɟɯn ʑo tɯrme ra pjɯ-nɯβle qhe, nɤkinɯ, tu-nɯkhrambe ntsɯ pjɤ-ŋu tɕe, 
nɯnɯra kɯ tɕe tɯrme kɯ-ŋɤn nɯ feilong to-sɤrmi-nɯ.
tɕendɤre bolaomu cho feilong nɯnɯ, nɤkinɯ iɕqha nɯ, ndʑi-tʂɤzɤn ra to-ndo-ndʑi, 
iɕqha tshɤndʐi ɣɯ tɯcifkɯm ra to-ndo-ndʑi, ɯ-ŋgɯ tɯ-ci ra chɤ-rku-ndʑi,
nɯra to-fkur-ndʑi qhe, 
ndʑi-tʂu nɯra wuma ʑo pjɤ-k-ɤrqhi-ci chondɤre, maka, 
iɕqha shamo nɯ, nɤki, qambɯt ʁɟa ʑo kɯ-jɯ-jom ʑo ju-ɕe-ndʑi pjɤ-ra.
tɕendɤre iɕqhanɯ, nɯ pjɤ-ra tɕe tɯ-sŋi tɕe tɕendɤre, 
nɯtɕu ko-k-ɤmdzɯ-ndʑi tɕe nɤki, nɯra ndʑi-tʂɤzɤn tɤ-kɤ-ndo cho ndʑi-tɯ-ci tɤ-kɤ-ndo nɯra pjɤ-k-ɤsɯ-ndzɤtshi-ndʑi-ci.
tɕeri feilong nɯ kɯ bolaomu ɯ-ɕki to-ti:
"a-xtɤɣ, nɤʑo, aʑo pjɯ-sɯso-a pɯ-ŋu nɤ, 
tɕiʑo ni ʁnaʁna ʑo pjɯ-ɴqa-tɕi, tɕi-xɕɤt tu-lɤt-tɕi mɯ́j-ra tɕe, 
tɕi-xɕɤt tu-lɤt-tɕi iɕqha,
tɕi-mtso cho tɯ-ci nɯ ʁnaʁna kɯ tu-fkur-tɕi mɯ́j-ra tɕe,
nɤʑo, nɤʑo ɣɯ nɤ-phoŋbu nɯ, nɤ-ɲɤm ɲɯ-khe,  qhendɤre, 
ɲɯ-tɯ-ɲur tɕe, aʑo tu-ti-a tɕe, nɤki, kɯ-mɤku nɤʑɯɣ nɯra ndza-tɕi, (150)
nɤ-kɤndza ra ndza-tɕi, nɤ-tɯ-ci ra tshi-tɕi tɕe,
tɕe nɯ nɤ-fkur nɯ tɯ-sŋi sɤz tɯ-sŋi/a/ a-tɤ-ʑo tɕe tɕendɤre,
nɤʑɯɣ nɯ tɯ-ɕkɯt-tɕi tɕe tɕe, ndɤre aʑɯɣ nɯ ndza-tɕi ɲɯ-sɯsam-a" to-ti.
tɕeri bolaomu nɯ kɯ ci nɯ maka mɯ-to-nɯsɯmŋɤn tɕe, tɕendɤre,
"nɯnɯ koŋla aʑo a-kɯ-pe tu-ti ɲɯ-ŋu" ɲɤ-sɯso qhe (215)
ɯ-ku to-sɤŋoʁŋoʁ qhe to-nɤla.
tɕe tɯ-sŋi tɕe tɕendɤre, nɤki, bolaomu ɣɯ ɯ-tɯ-ci chondɤre ɯ-tʂɤzɤn lonba to-so.
to-so tɕe tɕendɤre feilong ɯ-ɕki to-ti tɕe, 
aʑɯɣ nɯra, nɤki, kɤndza thɯ-arɕo ɕti tɕe, 
tɕe aʑo kɯnɤ, nɤki, nɤ-mtso cho nɤ-tɯ-ci nɯ kɯ-xtɕɯ-xtɕi ci ɲɯ-kɯ-jtshi-a raʁmaʁ" to-ti,
tɕeri feilong kɯ "aʑo, nɤkinɯ, iɕqha nɯ, jɤɣ ri, 
aʑɯɣ, iɕqhanɯ, a-qɤjɣi ɯ-qiɯ chondɤre tɯ-ci tɯ-mɯm nɯ kɯ nɤ-mɲaʁ ɯ-ntsi nɯ cho nɯ-sɤndu-tɕi ra" to-ti.
