kɤndʑɯsqhaj ɲɯ-kɯ-mɤrʑaβ kɤ-ti ɲɯ-ŋu.
tɕe kɯɕɯŋgɯ tɕe shandong ɣɯ ɯ-sɤtɕha yexian 掖县 ɲɯ-rmi.
nɯnɯtɕu nɤkinɯ, kha tɯtɯpɯ kɯ-ŋgɯ tsa ci pjɤ-tu.
tɕe nɯnɯ wuma ʑo pjɤ-ŋgɯ tɕe, nɯnɯ iɕqha nɯ, nɯ ɯ-kha nɯra nɯ-xing nɯ mao pjɤ-ŋu.
tɕe nɯnɯ rgɤtpu nɯnɯ /kɯ/, nɤkinɯ /kɯ/ pjɤ-ŋgɯ ɕti tɕe, 
kɯβʁa ra ɣɯ, nɯ-jla kɤ-lɤɣ kɯ, nɯnɯ kɯ-fse, nɤki, ɯ-kha chɯ-tɕɤt pjɤ-ŋu. (0030 instr)
tɕe nɯ ɯ-taʁ ku-rɤʑi-nɯ pjɤ-ra.
tɕendɤre nɤkinɯ iɕqha nɯ, kɤtsa ra rcanɯ, nɯ-kha mɤ-kɯ-pɯ-pe ʑo tɕe iɕqha, 
mbrɤz ɯ-ru kɯ kha thɯ-kɤ-sɯfkaβ kɯ-fse ɯ-ŋgɯ nɯtɕu ku-rɤʑi-nɯ pjɤ-ra tɕe wuma ʑo pjɤ-ŋgɯ-nɯ.
\c head-internal
tɕe nɯtɕu tɕendɤre iɕqha nɯ, nɯtɕu zɯ kɯβʁa ci, tshupa ɯ-ŋgɯ tɕe kɯβʁa ci, ɯ-xing zhang kɯ-ŋu ci pjɤ-tu tɕe,
nɯnɯra rca, wuma ʑo pjɤ-mɤɕi-nɯ tɕe,
tɕendɤre nɯ-kha ɯ-rkɯ nɯtɕu iɕqha, /nɯ-xing mao kɯ-ŋu ɣɯ, kɯ-ŋgɯ ra ɣ/, nɯtɕu wuma ʑo sɤtɕha kɯ-pe ci to-nɯ-qɤr tɕe,
nɯnɯtɕu ɯ-tɯrsa, 
kɤtsa ra nɯ ɯ-qhu ɣɯ tɕe, nɯ-tɯrsa kɯ-ŋu nɯ, to-nɯ-sɯ-qaʁ-nɯ. (0120)
nɯnɯ to-sɯ-qaʁ-nɯ tɕe nɯnɯ to-sɯβzu-nɯ.
tɕeri /tɯ/ mɯ-tonɤrʑaʁ ma, kɯ-sɤmtshɤr ci pjɤ-tu ma, 
tɯrme ra kɯ tu-ti-nɯ tɕe,
"ɯnɯnɯtɕu, iɕqha zhangjia ra ɣɯ nɯ-tɯrsa ɯ-rkɯ nɯtɕu tu-kɯ-ŋke tɕe, 
ɲɯ-kɯ-sɤŋo tɕe, tɯrme kɯ-rɯɕmi kɯ-fse ɣɤʑu" to-ti-nɯ.
tɕendɤre zhangjia nɯra, nɯnɯ iɕqha maojia nɯra ɣɯ, nɯ-kha nɯ nɤki, nɯ-sɤtɕha nɯnɯ, a-mɤ-nɯ-zrɯ-nɯ ɲɯ-ra"
tɕe "a-mɤ-nɯ-tɯ-zrɯ-nɯ tɕe ʑatsa jɤ-cit-nɯ ma nɯ mɯ́j-pe" to-ti-nɯ.
tɕendɤre nɯ ɯ-qhu tɕe tɕe nɤki, zhangjia nɯra ɣɯ, nɤkinɯ, (nɯ)-kha nɯtɕu mɤ-kɯ-pe ci ntsɯ pjɤ-tu.
tɯrme kɯ-si kɯ-fse, nɯ-kɯ-mŋɤm kɯ-thɯ kɯ-ndzoʁ kɯ-fse,
qhe nɤki, kɯmaʁ mɤ-kɯ-pe kɯ-fse pjɤ-xcat ʑo.
tɕendɤre iɕqha nɯ, nɤkinɯ, nɯnɯ nɯ-tɯrsa ɯ-sta nɯ jo-scɤt-nɯ pjɤ-ra.
tɕe nɯnɯ nɯ-tɯrsa ɯ-sta nɯ jo-scɤt-nɯ pjɤ-ra tɕe,
nɯ ja-scɤt-nɯ tɕe, ɯ-sta nɯtɕu, maka qawoʁ ci kɯ-rnɯ-rnaʁ ʑo pjɤ-tu.
matɕi iɕqha tɯrsa nɯ to-tɕɤt-nɯ tɕe jo-tsɯm-nɯ.
tɕe tɯ-sŋi tɕe iɕqha, maogong nɯnɯ li, nɤki, jla ɯ-kɯ-lɤɣ jo-ɕe tɕe tɕendɤre,
nɯnɯtɕu iɕqha, zhangjia ra ɣɯ nɯ-tɯrsa ɯ-rkɯ nɯtɕu jɤ-azɣɯt ʑo tɕe tɕendɤre,
tɯmɯ qale kɯ ɲɤ-sɯxcat ʑo. (240)
tɕendɤre iɕqha nɯ, tɯmɯ kɯ-wxtɯ-wxti ʑo ko-lɤt.
tɕendɤre iɕqha maogong nɯnɯ, kɯ, maka ɯ-z-nɤprɯ ra ɲɤ-ɕar ri,
maka, nɤkinɯ, ɯ-z-nɤprɯ ɯ-jaʁ mɯ-pjɤ-ɣi.
\c oblique participle
tɯ-mɯ ri maka mɯ-to-znɯna qhe, nɯ sɤznɤ (300 tr? tɯmɯ)
pjɯ-lɤt ɯ-jɯja pɯ-pjɯ-wxti ʑo pjɤ-ɕti qhe, (305)
tɯ-mɯ ɯ-tɯ-wxti kɯ iɕqha, maogong nɯnɯ nɤki iɕqha, nɯnɯ tɯrsa jɤ-kɤ-scɤt ɣɯ ɯ-sta, qawoʁ ɯ-ŋgɯ nɯtɕu pjɤ́-wɣ-tsɯm.
tɕe pjɤ́-wɣ-tsɯm qhe tɕendɤre, nɤki, 
kɯ-dɤn mɯ-to-tsu qhe, maogong nɯ nɯ ɯ-ŋgɯ nɯtɕu 
ɲɤ́-wɣ-sɯmtshɯβ qhe pjɤ-si. (320)
pjɤ-si qhe tɕe nɯtɕu ʑo kó-wɣ-zrɤʑi qhe, (325 inv?)
nɯre iɕqha, zhangjia ra kɯ nɯra pa-sɯz-nɯ ɯ-qhu qhe, nɤki,
zhangjia ra ɣɯ nɯ-jmŋo ci pjɤ-tu wo, iɕqha, 
zhangjia tɤ-wa nɯ ɣɯ, ɯ-jmŋo ci ko-βzu qhe tɕendɤre,  (340)
ɯ-jmŋo ci pjɤ-tu qhe, nɯnɯ to-ʁjit qhe tɕendɤre, nɤkinɯ,
rŋɯl to-tɕɤt qhe iɕqha, maogong nɯ nɯtɕu pjɤ-sɯskɯ.
nɯ ɯ-sta nɯtɕu pjɤ-sɯskɯ.
tɕendɤre ɯ-rʑaβ chondɤre ɯ-tɕɯ nɯnɯra kha jo-sɯɣe.
tɕe nɯra to-nɯβdaʁ.
tɕendɤre to-nɯβdaʁ qhendɤre,
zhanggong nɯnɯ, nɯnɯra nɯ-wa nɯ kɯ iɕqha, maolang nɯ pa-mto rca wuma ʑo pjɤ-nɯrga.
tɕe ɯ-taʁ wuma ʑo pjɤ-pe tɕe tɕendɤre pjɤ-zrɤβzjoz.
tɕendɤre nɯ sɤznɤ, ʑaʑa ʑo qhe tɕe, nɤkinɯ, ɯ-me kɯ-wxti nɯ ɯ-rʑaβ ɲɤ-kho.
tɕeri nɤkinɯ, iɕqha, zhanggong nɯnɯ, zhangjia ra ɣɯ nɯ-me kɯ-wxti nɯ kɯ "iɕqha maolang nɯnɯ kɯ-ŋgɯ ɯ-rɟit ɲɯ-ɕti" to-ti qhe,
maka ʑo /kɤ kɤznɤla/ ɯ-rʑaβ kɤ-sɯβzu mɯ-pjɤ-khɯ. (430 sɯ?)
tɕendɤre mɯ-pjɤ-khɯ qhe, zhanggong nɯ kɯ, ɯ-kɤpa ɲɤ-me qhe, nɤkinɯ,
ɯ-me kɯ-xtɕi nɯ ɯ-ɕki "nɤ-pi nɯ-nɯkhɤde ra" to-ti.
tɕendɤre nɯnɯ ɯ-ʁi nɯ kɯ "a-pi nɤʑo haishi, nɤkinɯ, nɯ sɤznɤ a-wa kɯ ta-tɯt nɯ tɤ-ste wo tɕe, nɤkinɯ, 
maolang nɯnɯ ɯ-ʁzɯɣ ri ɲɯ-pe, ɯ-kɤ-spa ri ɲɯ-dɤn ɕti tɕe,
ɯ-qhu tɕe nɤʑɯɣ kɯ-pe ci βze ɕti tɕe, tɯ́-wɣ-sɯscit ɕti tɕe "ɣa" tɤ-ti. (505)
tɕeri tɤ-pi nɯ kɯ nɯ ko-sɤŋo mɤra ma, nɯ sɤznɤ "qhe nɯ nɯsthɯci ɯ-ɲɯ́-pa nɤ, nɤʑo tɕhindʐa ɯ-rʑaβ mɤ-tɯ-βze" to-ti.
tɕeri, tɤ-pi nɯ kɯ nɯ ta-tɯt jamar tɕe, ɯ-ʁi nɯ kɯ "wo jɤɣ ma, aʑo ɯ-rʑaβ ku-βze-a jɤɣ" to-ti.
tɕendɤre nɯnɯ /kɯr/ kɯ-xtɕi nɯ kɯ ɯ-rʑaβ ko-βzu tɕe, tɕe ndɤre, (525)
maolang kɯ pa-sɯz ɯ-qhu tɕe wuma ʑo ɲɤ-nɤxtʂɯn.
