tɕendɤre @caichen nɯ kɯ wuma ʑo li nɯ pjɤ-nɤsɤɣdɯɣ ri,
tɕe nɯ li nɯ nɯ-rca ku-rɤʑi kɯ-khɯ nɯra pjɤ-rga qhe,

tɕe ɕɤr qhe tɕendɤre nɤki, @caichen ɣɯ, nɤki, ɯ-snaχtsa nɯra ɲɯ-ndɯl. nɯra pjɤ-ŋu.
tɕe nɤki, ɯ-sɲɯɣjɯ ra chɯ-χtɕi, nɯra pjɤ-ŋu.
tɕendɤre nɯnɯtɕu tɕendɤre, 
nɯtɕu sɤtɕha kɯ-kɯ-rɤʑi nɯ, kha kɯ-kɯ-rɤʑi nɯra kɯ, @xiaoqian nɯ wuma ʑo tɕheme ɲɯ-pe, ɲɯ-sɤsɤŋo,
nɯra, nɤkinɯ, wuma ʑo mɯ́j-nɯpɤɴqi, nɯra tu-ti-nɯ pjɤ-ŋu tɕe tɕe,
ʑɯrɯʑɤri tɕe tɕe iɕqha, @caichen ɣɯ ɯ-mu nɯ kɯ li @xiaoqian nɯ ɲɤ-nɯrga.
tɕendɤre ci ku-pa-ndʑi to-nɤla.
tɕe nɯ ɯ-qhu tɕe kɤtsa ra kɯ-scɯ-scit  ku-nɯ-rɤʑi-nɯ pjɤ-ŋu. (1255 rcɯ)
tɕe nɯnɯ jamar tɕe tɕendɤre, iɕqha nɯ, 
iɕqha laoyao tu-ti-nɯ, kɯ-sɤsat tɤ-mu, ɬɤndʐi tɤ-mu nɯnɯ kɯ, @xiaoqian nɯ wuma ʑo, nɤkinɯ, ɯ-mbrɯ pjɤ-ɕɯŋgɯ. (1310 erreur @xiaoqian kɯ tɤ-mu nɯ ɯ-mbrɯ pjɤ-ɕɯŋgɯ?)
tɕendɤre iɕqha nɯ, nɯni tɤ-rɯstɯnmɯ-ndʑi,
ɯ-fsomɯr tɕe tɕe iɕqha, nɤki, tɤ-mu nɯ, srɯnmɯ tɤ-mu nɯnɯ, tɯrme ra nɯ-se chɯ-kɯ-nɤtshɯtshi nɯ, li kukutɕu nɯ-kha zɯ jo-ɣi.
tɕe nɯ, tɕe "kɯki kɤtsa ra ɲɯ-ɣɤme-a ra" ɲɤ-sɯso.
tɕeri iɕqha @xiaoqian nɯ ʑaʑa ʑo nɯnɯ ɯ-ftɕaka tu-βze, tu-rɯndzaŋspa pjɤ-ɕti.
tɕe nɯnɯ to-rɯftɕaka ɕti tɕe tɕe, (1340 calque)
caichen ɯ-ɕki "yanchixia kɯ nɯ-tɯ́-wɣ-mbi nɯnɯ scapa ɯ-fkɯm nɯnɯ, kha tɕe tɤ-ɕɯɴqoʁ" to-ti,
"tɕetu khɤrka tɕe tɤ-ɕɯɴqoʁ ra" to-ti.
tɕendɤre iɕqha, @qiannian @laoyao 前年老妖, tɕe iɕqha srɯnmɯ nɯ ɯ-stoŋtsu-xpa tɤ-kɯ-tsu pjɤ-ɕti tɕe, 
tɕeri nɯnɯ ɯ-scapa nɯnɯ, ɯ-fkɯm nɯnɯ, tɕe tɤ-kɤ-ɕɯɴqoʁ nɯ mɯ-pjɤ-sɯz.
tɕendɤre, ndɤre, nɯ fse ʑo kha ɯ-ŋgɯ lo-ɣi jamar ri tɕe, 
tɕendɤre nɯɕɯmɯma ʑo, nɤki, tɤ́-wɣ-rɤwum qhe, scapa ɯ-fkɯm ɯ-ŋgɯ nɯtɕu chɤ́-wɣ-rku. (1420 calque)
tɕendɤre nɯnɯ kɤntɕhɯ-xpa nɯ-ari ɯ-qhu tɕe tɕendɤre,
iɕqha, @caichen nɯ kɯ @gaozhong, nɤki, pjɤ-cha,
kɤ-ɕe pjɤ-cha qhe tɕendɤre, wuma ʑo, nɤkinɯ, ɯ-khɯr kɯ-wxti to-nɯ-ndo.
