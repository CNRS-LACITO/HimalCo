χpi ki ɣɯ ɯ-rmi nɯ laoshan daoshi 崂山道士 kɤ-ti ɲɯ-ŋu. daoshi. 
kɯɕɯŋgɯ tɕe, nɤkinɯ, ɯ-rmi wang 王 kɯ-ŋu ci, kɯ-rɤβzjoz ci, tɤ-pɤtso ci pjɤ-tu tɕe, 
ɯʑo ɯ-kha nɯnɯ rcanɯ, mɲɯtsi skalpa ʑo nɯ, nɤkinɯ, jɯɣi kɯ-nɤmɤle ntsɯ pjɤ-ŋu.
tɕe nɯnɯ kɤ-rɤβzjoz nɯra wuma pjɤ-χɕu-nɯ tɕe, nɤkinɯ,
nɯ-kha nɯra wuma ʑo, nɤkinɯ, pjɤ-k-ɤchɯcha-nɯ-ci pjɤ-mɤɕi-nɯ,
tɕendɤre kɯki wangsheng 王生 nɯnɯ sɤ-xtɕɯ-xtɕi jɤznɤ, nɤkinɯ, ɯ-ŋga chondɤre ɯ-kɤndza nɯra kɤ-nɯzdɯɣ /mɤ/ kɤ-nɤrkɯn mɤ-kɯ-ra pjɤ-ŋu.
tɕendɤre iɕqha nɯ, ɯ-ŋga tɤ-ra tɕe ɯ-jaʁ ju-ɕthɯz, 
ɯ-ndzɤtshi tɤ-ra tɕe, ɯ-mtɕhi tu-ci ma nɯ ma mɤ-kɯ-ra kɯ-fse nɯ pjɤ-ŋu tɕe tɕeri, (050)
ɲɯ-sɤŋo tɕe iɕqha laoshan nɯtɕu zɯ, wuma ʑo χpɯn, nɤkinɯ, sŋaʁspa tsa ci, wuma ʑo kɯ-cha ci pjɤ-tu ɲɯ-ŋu.
tɕe nɯ ɯ-fashu nɯnɯra rcanɯ, nɯ ɲɯ-fse, nɯ ɲɯ-raŋ, nɯra tu-nɤme ɲɯ-cha, 
nɯra ntsɯ kɤ-ti pjɯ-mtshɤm pjɤ-ŋu.
tɕendɤre nɯ ɲɤ-nɯrga tɕe, "nɯnɯtɕu tu-ɕe-a tɕe ɕ-pjɯ-βzjoz-a ra" ntsɯ ɲɯ-sɯsɤm pjɤ-ŋu.
tɕe ɯ-jmŋo ʑo ku-ntɕhɤr pjɤ-ŋu.
tɕendɤre tɯ-xpa ɲɤ-k-ɤrɕo-ci ri, ki wangsheng ki, nɤki, ko-spa maka pjɤ-me, kha nɯtɕu. (120)
tɕe ɯ-laχtɕha nɯra to-sɤpɯpa tɕe,
tɕe iɕqha laoshan kɤ-ti zgoku nɯtɕu, nɯnɯ sloχpɯn ɯ-kɯ-ɕar ɕe pjɤ-ŋu.
tɕendɤre to-rɤŋgat tɕe tɕendɤre, nɯnɯ ɯ-sŋi nɯtɕu tɕe kɯki iɕqha, wangsheng nɯnɯ laoshan nɯtɕu, zgoku nɯtɕu to-zɣɯt.
to-zɣɯt tɕe tɕeri, nɤki, ju-ru tɕe, mɤ-kɯ-ɤrqhi ci zɯ, nɤkinɯ, kha ci pjɤ-tu tɕe, nɯtɕu, nɤkinɯ, tɤkhɯ pjɤ-ɬoʁ, tɤkhɯ ra sɲiŋɯŋi ʑo pjɤ-ɬoʁ. (200)
tɕendɤre nɤkinɯ, /ɯ-ŋgɯ lo/ kha ɯ-ŋgɯ lo-ɕe.
lo-ɕe tɕe nɯnɯ, nɤki, nɯnɯ kha nɯnɯ ɯ-ŋgɯ nɯtɕu, kha kɯ-jɯ-jom ci pjɤ-tu tɕe, 
nɯtɕu rgɤtpu ɯ-mtɕhi /thɯ/ ɯ-rme thɯ-kɯ-wɣrum ci pjɤ-k-ɤmzdɯ-ci. (210)
tɕe nɯ ɯ-khri ɯ-taʁ nɯtɕu pjɤ-k-ɤmzdɯ-ci tɕendɤre lu-ru tɕendɤre,
wuma ʑo pjɤ-ɣɤʁre ɲɯ-ŋu.
tɕendɤre lu-ru tɕe, fsraŋma ci ʑo pjɤ-fse ɲɯ-ŋu.
tɕendɤre nɤki tɤ-wɯ nɯnɯ ci pjɤ-rɯkhɤcɤl ri tɕe, nɤkinɯ, 
tɤ-pɤtso nɯ kɯ ku-rtoʁ tɕe iɕqha tɤ-wɯ nɯ,
tɯ-mɯ ta-fɕɤt nɤ, tɯ-mɯ ɯ-pɕoʁ ɲɯ-tso, (230 directional; -nɯ nɤ?)
sɤtɕha pa-fɕɤt nɤ, sɤtɕha ɯ-pɕoʁ ɲɯ-tso,
tɕendɤre iɕqha li, nɤki, fanzheng,
mɤ-spe ʑo /pjɯ/ kɯ-me ci pjɤ-ŋu. (248)
nɯ tɤ-stu-t-a, nɯnɯ tɤ-pa-t-a, nɯra tu-ti pjɤ-ŋu tɕe,  
tɕe wangsheng kɯ "waŋ" wuma ʑo ɲɤ-rga,
ndɤre "a-khi ma kɯki koŋla ɲɤ-k-ɤtɯɣ-a-ci" ɲɤ-sɯso. (250)
tɕendɤre ɯ-χpɯm pjɤ-tshoʁ tɕe tɕendɤre, 
"wortɕhi ʑo aʑo nɤ-slama ku-kɯ-wum-a, tu-kɯ-sɯβzu-a ra" to-ti. (300 calque?)
tɕe nɤʑo ɣɯ, nɤkinɯ, nɤ-sŋaʁ nɯra, ɣɯ nɯra, ftɕaka ra lonba pjɯ-kɯ-sɯxɕat-a ra" to-ti.
tɕeri, nɯnɯ daozhang 道长 nɯnɯ ɲɤ-nɤre.
"aʑo ku-ta-rtoʁ tɕe, ma, nɤ-ɕa ta ɯ-tɯ-mpɯ-mpɯ kɯ, nɯnɯra, kɯki kɯ-fse ra, kɯ-ɴqɯ-ɴqa ɕti tɕe, tɯ-cha mɤ-tɯ-cha mɤxsi, 
aʑo "tɯ-cha" mɯ́j-sɯsam-a" to-ti.
tɕendɤre wangsheng nɯ kɯ pa-mtshɤm tɕe, kɯjŋu to-joʁ,
"aʑo nɤki, iɕqha nɤʑɯɣ nɤ-kɤ-spa nɯnɯra thamtɕɤt nɯ, tɯ-sŋaʁ ra kɤ-sɯspa a-pɯ-tɯ-cha qhe,  (335)
aʑo rcanɯ kɯki nɤ-slama ki rca, 
tɕhi kɯ-fse pɯ-nnɯ-sɤɣdɯɣ kɯnɤ, nɤɕqe-a ʑo, kɤ-nɤɕqa cha-a" to-ti.
tɕendɤre maka mɯ-to-khɯ qhendɤre daozhang nɯ kɯ zdɯxthɯɣ ʑo to-nɤla
tɕendɤre ɯ-fsosoz tɕendɤre, tɯmtɕi ʑo tɕe tɕendɤre iɕqha, 
daozhang nɯ kɯ, nɤkinɯ, wangsheng cho kɯmaʁ ɯ-slama nɯnɯra, nɤkinɯ, to-zrɤŋgat.
ma ɯ-ɕki jo-sɯɣe tɕe, /tɕeki/ (tɕetu) zgoku tɤ-ɕe-nɯ tɕe "si ʑ-lɤ-phɯt-nɯ ra" to-ti. (410)
tɕendɤre ku-rtoʁ tɕe wangsheng nɯ kɯ stu kɯ-mɤku ʑo tɯ-rpa to-ndo tɕe, zgo ɯ-taʁ to-ɕe rcanɯ, wuma ʑo pjɤ-stu.
tɕendɤre nɯ kɯ-fse nɯnɯ tɯ-sla to-tsu ri, tɕe wangsheng nɯ maka kɤ-nɤɕqa mɯ-pjɤ-cha.
tɕendɤre iɕqha ɯ-jaʁ ri, nɤki, iɕqha nɯ, cɯmbɤrom mɯ́j-ʑɯ nɤ, 
/nɤkinɯ tɤrkhɤz ʑo ko-ta./ 
ta-ʁrɯ ʑo ko-ta.
tɕendɤre, tɕe nɯ jamar tɕe daltsɯtsa tɕe tɕe kɤ-nɯɕe tsa ɯ-sɯm to-ɣi. (445)
\c complementation
tɕendɤre tɯ-rʑaʁ tɕendɤre, wangsheng nɯ kɯ iɕqha nɯ, ku-rtoʁ tɕe iɕqha, daozhang nɯ ɣɯ, nɤki, ɯ-βzaŋsa tsuku jo-ɣi-nɯ.
tɕendɤre, nɤkinɯ iɕqha nɯ, nɯnɯtɕu jo-ɣi-nɯ tɕe tɕendɤre, 
cha ra ko-tshi-nɯ pjɤ-rɯkhɤcɤl-nɯ tɕe tɕendɤre, 
tɯrmɯ kɤ-ɣe, kɤ-qanɯ tɕe tɕendɤre, 
daozhang nɯ kɯ, tɯrme ra, nɤkinɯ, tɤtʂu, lazhu ra mɯ-to-sɯzwɤr kɯ, nɤkinɯ, tsɯntu to-ndo tɕe ɕoʁɕoʁ kɯ-ɤrtɯm ci ɲɤ-βzu.
ɕoʁɕoʁ kɯ-ɤrtɯm ci to-qrɯ.
tɕendɤre to-qrɯ tɕe tɕendɤre nɯnɯ, znde ɯ-taʁ ko-ɲɟoʁ.
tɕe nɯ kha ɣɯ ɯ-znde ɯ-taʁ nɯtɕu ko-ɲɟoʁ tɕe,
toʁde ʑo ma mɯ-to-tsu ma tɕe nɯnɯ, nɯnɯ ɕoʁɕoʁ kɯ-ɤrtɯm kɤ-kɤ-ɲɟoʁ nɯ, sla kɯ-ɤrtɯ-rtɯm ʑo ɲɤ-k-ɤβzu-ci tɕe,
wuma ʑo pjɤ-fsoʁ.
tɕendɤre nɤki, kha ɣɯ ɯ-kɤχcɤl nɯtɕu ʑo to-ɕe tɕe rcanɯ,
ɯ-sŋi ʑo kɯ-fse kɯ-fsoʁ ɲɤ-sɤβzu. (545)
tɕe toʁde tɕe, nɤki, ɯ-ndʐuwa tɯ-rdoʁ nɯ kɯ to-ti tɕe,
"nɯ, wuma ʑo nɯnɯ, maka, nɤ-kɤ-spa ɲɯ-dɤn tɕe, nɤ-sŋaʁ nɯ ɲɯ-khɯ nɤ, wuma ʑo,
nɯnɯ wuma ʑo ɲɯ-mbre tɕe ɲɯ-pe tɕe,
tɕe tham tɕe sla to-tu ɕti tɕe, 
sla nɯ sthɯci kɯ-fsoʁ to-tu tɕe,
/tɕhindʐa nɯ/ iɕqha, chang'e xianzi 嫦娥 nɯ, 
ɯnɯnɯ tɕheme nɯ, chang'e kɤ-ti nɯnɯ, tɯmɯ kɤrŋi ɯ-me nɯnɯ, jɤ-tɯ-sɯɣe-t tɕe tɕetha mɤʑɯ sɤscit ɲɯ-ŋu" to-ti. (615)
tɕendɤre daozhang nɯ kɯ ɯ-ku ra to-sɤŋoʁŋoʁ tɕe "ɲɯ-ŋu" to-ti.
tɕendɤre /nɯm/ nɯɕɯmɯma ʑo tɕe iɕqha, ɯ-ʁɤri ndʑu tɯ-tɕha nɯnɯ, nɤki, tɯ-ldʑa nɯnɯ to-joʁ.
