kɯ-nɤphɯphɯ fsraŋma kɤ-ti ɲɯ-ŋu.
/kɯmnɤ/ (kɯ-nɤphɯphɯ) fsraŋma kɤ-ti ci ɲɯ-ŋu tɕe, 
kɯki tɯrme gao yucheng 高玉成 kɯ-rmi ci pjɤ-tu tɕe,
nɯnɯ kha nɯra wuma ʑo li kɯ-ɤpɯpa tsa, nɯ-rmi ra kɯ-tu ci pjɤ-ɕti-nɯ. (010)
tɕe nɯ ɯʑo kɯ smɤnba kɤ-βzu wuma pjɤ-mkhɤz, 
tɕendɤre nɤki, smɤnba tu-βze chondɤre iɕqha nɯ, tɯrme ɣɯ ɯ-kɯ-pe kɤ-βzu nɯra wuma ʑo pjɤ-cha. (025)
ɕɯ ɯ-ɕki pɯ-nɯ-ŋɯ-ŋu ʑo, koŋla kɯ-pɯ-pe ʑo, ɯ-kɤ-cha nɯ tɕhi kɯ-tu ʑo nɯ tu-ste??? tɕe tu-sɯqur pjɤ-ŋgrɤl. (030)
tɯ-sŋi tɕe, tɕendɤre, gao yucheng nɯ smɤnba ɯ-kɯ-βzu jo-ɕe tɕe, 
kɯm chɯ-tɯ-ɬoʁ ʑo tɕe, ɯ-kɯm ɯ-thɤcu iɕqha, jɤɣɤt ɯ-pa nɯtɕu, nɤki,
tɯrme rcanɯ, ji-kɯ-si tɤ-kɯ-rɤŋgat kɯ-fse ci maka, ɯ-sroʁ ra ji-kɯ-mbrɤt ʑo kɯ-fse ʑo kɯ-nɤphɯphɯ ci pjɤ-tu.
tɕendɤre ku-rtoʁ tɕe rcanɯ, ɯ-mi nɯra, ɯ-mi ɯ-ru nɯra rcanɯ, khɯtsa ɯ-mŋu jamar ???ɯ-kɯ-wxti tɯ-ɣmaz pjɤ-tu. (100)
tɕe ɯ-ŋgɯ tɕe nɤki, /tɤlu/ tɤ-spɯ cho tɤ-se ra pjɤ-nɯɬoʁ tɕe, 
ɯ-di kɯ-sɤjlɯ-jloʁ ʑo pjɤ-mnɤm.
tɕendɤre nɤki, gao yucheng nɯ kɯ nɯɕɯmɯma ʑo ɯ-ʁjoʁ ra nɯ-ɕki "kɯki kha ɯ-ŋgɯ ra lɤ-sco-nɯ ra" to-ti. (115 sco)

ɯʑo kefang 客房 ɣɯ ɯ-χcɤl nɯtɕu /akɤ/ a-kɤ-tɯ-ɕɯrŋgɯ-nɯ" to-ti.
tɕe nɯ ɯʑo smɤnba ɯ-kɯ-βzu jo-ɕe tɕe tɕendɤre,
jɤ-azɣɯt tɕe tɕendɤre iɕqha nɯ, koŋla kɯ-pɯ-pe ʑo taqaβ ra to-lɤt to-nɯsmɤn.

tɕe tɕendɤre ɯ-ndzɤtshi nɯra wuma a-tɤ-tɯ-sɤpe-nɯ ra" to-ti.
tɕe laʁnɤ-rʑaʁ ɲɤ-ɕqhlɤt tɕe iɕqha, kɯ-nɤphɯphɯ nɯ hanɯni to-mna.
