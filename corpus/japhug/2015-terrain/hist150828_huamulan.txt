ɯ-me nɯ koŋla ci ko-nɤrtɯrtoʁ tɕe tɕendɤre, ɯ-qom ra rɯβnɤrɯβ ʑo pjɤ-ɣi.
tɕe ɯ-mtɕhi tɕe to-ti, "wo ɲɯ-pe, ɲɯ-pe, jɤ-tɯ-nɯɣe qhe, (1130)
tɕendɤre jɤ-tɯ-nɯɣe qhe, ɲɯ-pe" nɯra to-ti.
tɕe ɯ-ʁi nɯ kɯ ɯ-pi pa-mto tɕe tɕe, mɤʑɯ ʑo ɲɤ-rga,
tɕe sqɯ-xpa ɯ-ro to-tsu qhe, nɯ ɯ-ʁi ndɤre wuma ʑo chɤ-wxti tɕe, 
koŋla ʑo ʁʑɯnɯ ɲɤ-k-ɤβzu-ci tɕe, 
ɯ-pi jo-nɯɣi pjɤ-sɯz tɕe tɕe ʑaʑa ʑo, nɤki, paʁ pjɤ-ntɕha, qaʑo ra pjɤ-ntɕha qhe, 
tɕendɤre nɯra cha cho nɯra, cai nɯra to-mɲo.
tɕe ɯ-z-nɤjo ra to-βzu qhe tɕendɤre,
kɯki pɯ-rɯndzɤtshi-nɯ ɯ-qiɯ jamar tɤ-rʑaʁ nɯ-ari tɕe, (1210)
huamulan ɯ-pi nɯ kɯ huamulan lo-tsɯm, tɕe kha ɯ-ŋgɯ lo-tsɯm lo-sɯɣe tɕe,
tɕendɤre nɤki, iɕqha ʁmaʁmi ɯ-ŋga nɯra ɲɤ-sɯ-tɕɤt.
tɕendɤre koŋla tɕheme ɯ-ŋga to-ʑŋga.
tɕendɤre, huamulan nɯ kɯ-fse tɕheme ɯ-ŋga tɤ-kɯ-ŋga nɯ chɤ-nɯɬoʁ tɕe,
ɯ-mu ɯ-wa ni ndʑi-ɕki li, maka nɤkinɯ, ɯ-pɕaʁ ra pjɤ-βzu qhe, nɤkinɯ,
nɯra to-rɯɕmi qhe tɕe, ɯ-mu ɯ-wa ni ndʑi-ɕki tɕe nɤkinɯ, 
"aʑo ndʑi-ɕki ci jɤ-nɯɣe-a" nɯra to-ti.
tɕendɤre mulan ɣɯ ɯ-zda ra kɯ ɲɤ-nɤmtshɤr-nɯ, ɲɤ-nɯcaχto-nɯ.
"tɕhi ɯ-skɤt ɲɯ-ŋu, /tɕhi/ ki tɕhi pjɯ-fse ɲɯ-ŋu" nɯra to-ti-nɯ ɲɯ-ŋu. (1255)
tɕeri huamulan kɯ nɯra pɯ-kɯ-fse qhe,
ɯ-wa thɯ-kɯ-rgɤz, ɯ-ʁi ri kɯ-xtɕi qhe tɕe, nɯ ma kɤpa pjɤ-me tɕe tɕe, 
núndʐa a-wa ɯ-sci ʁmaʁ jɤ-ari-a tɕetha (1300)
iʑo ji-rɟɤlkhɤβ ɯ-kɯ-fsraŋ nɯra jɤ-ari-a pɯ-ŋu" nɯra to-ti qhe,
tɕendɤre ɯ-zda ra nɯ-sɤŋo-nɯ qhe, "wo" nɯ wuma ʑo ɲɤ-nɤmtshɤr-nɯ qhe, (1315 ɲɯ ɲɤ)
"nɯ ɯ-tɯ-cha nɯ! nɯ rcanɯ wuma ʑo, nɤkinɯ li iʑo, nɤki rɟɤlkhɤβ ɣɯ ɯ-tɕheme kɯ-cha ci chɤ-ɬoʁ ɲɯ-ɕti" nɯra to-ti-nɯ ɲɯ-ŋu.
