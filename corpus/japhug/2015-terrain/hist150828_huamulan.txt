tɕendɤre ɯ-wa nɯ kɯnɤ wuma ʑo tɤlɯlɤt kɯ-ʂa ci tɕe, yingxiong pjɤ-ɕti nɤ, wuma ʑo kɯ-cha. (030, kɯʂa)
tɕeri huamulan nɯnɯ, nɤkinɯ, sɤ-xtɕɯ-xtɕi ʑo ɯ-wa ɯ-ɕki nɯ tɕe iɕqha, tɤlɯlɤt ɯ-tshɯɣa nɯra pjɤ-βzjoz.
tɕe nɯ ɯ-phoŋbu nɯra kɤ-sɯɣʑaʁ nɯra pjɤ-rɲo.

dongbei nɯnɯ tɕe li tɤlɯlɤt pjɤ-ra.
kɤ-ɤlɯlɤt pjɤ-ra tɕe tɕendɤre chaoting nɯ kɯ ʁmaʁ kɤ-ɕar ftɕaka to-βzu tɕe,
kɯkɯra ɣɯ nɯ-jilco nɯnɯra, tshupa nɯnɯ ɯ-ŋgɯ nɯra, tɤ-tɕɯ nɯ-lɯz kɯ-xtɕi thamtham thɯ-kɯ-cha kɯ-fse nɯra, (110 thamtham)
kɤsɯfse ʑo kɤ-ɤlɯlɤt jó-wɣ-tsɯm-nɯ, jo-ɕe-nɯ.
tɕeri huamulan ɯ-wa nɯ kɯnɤ ju-ɕe kɯ-ra pjɤ-ŋu ri, ɯ-lɯz chɤ-ɣi.
ɯ-lɯz chɤ-ɣi tɕe tɕendɤre, ɯ-mu nɯ kɯ, nɤkinɯ, chɤ-nɯzdɯɣ tɕe,
"ki ndɤre, maka, nɤ-wa nɯthamtɕɤt ʑo thɯ-wxti tɕe, (130)
nɯ kɯ-fse /kɯ/ nɤki, ɕɯ-kɤ-ɤlɯlɤt ndɤre, nɤki, cha ɕi kɯma, ɲɯ-sɤnɯzdɯɣ" to-ti.
tɕe ɯ-pi nɯ li tɕheme pjɤ-ŋu tɕe, tɕe li ɯ-ku ra pjɤ-phɤβ tɕe,
"ɲɯ-ŋu tɕe, iʑo ji-kha tɕe tɕendɤre, thamtham ʑo kɯ-cha tɤ-tɕɯ maŋe tɕe, tɕhi tú-wɣ-pa ra kɯ?" to-ti. (150 prenom)
