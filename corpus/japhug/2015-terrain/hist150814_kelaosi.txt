kɯki ɯ-rmi, tɯrme ɯ-rmi nɯ, nɤki, kelaosi kɯ-xtɕi cho kelaosi kɯ-wxti tu-kɯ-ti ŋu.
tɕe kɯɕɯŋgɯ tɕe, nɤkinɯ, jɯl ci pjɤ-tu tɕe, nɯtɕu tɯrme ʁnɯz pjɤ-rɤʑi-ndʑi.
tɕe ʁnaʁna ʑo kelaosi pjɤ-rmi-ndʑi.
tɕendɤre ndʑi-kɤ-sɤmbrɤqɤt kɤ-nɯmga tɕe, ci nɯnɯ kelaosi kɯ-xtɕi, ci nɯ kelaosi kɯ-wxti to-sɤrmi-nɯ.
tɕendɤre nɤki, kelaosi kɯ-xtɕi nɯ wuma ʑo pjɤ-ŋgɯ tɕendɤre,
ɯ-mbro tɯ-rdoʁ ma pjɤ-me.
kelaosi kɯ-wxti nɯ kɯ pjɤ-mɤɕi tɕe, ɯ-mbro kɯβde pjɤ-tu.
\c comparative
tɕendɤre sɲikuku ʑo kelaosi kɯ-xtɕi nɯ /kɯ/, nɤkinɯ iɕqha nɯ, tu-ɕlu ntsɯ pjɤ-ra.
nɤki, kelaosi kɯ-wxti nɯ ɣɯ, ɕ-tu-qur tɕe ɕ-tu-ɕlu, 
tɕendɤre nɤki nɯnɯ, yigelibai kɯɕnɯ-sŋi to-tsu tɕe, stu kɯ-maqhu ɯ-sŋi nɯtɕu tɕe, tɕendɤre nɤkinɯ, kelaosi kɯ-xtɕi nɯ kɯ, iɕqha nɯ, mbro kɯmŋu nɯ ntsɯ ju-nɤm tɕe, nɤki,
ɯʑo ɯ-ji ɯ-ŋgɯ nɯtɕu tɕe ɕ-tu-nɯ-ɕlu pjɤ-ŋu, ɕ-tu-rɤma pjɤ-ŋu.
tɕendɤre iɕqha nɯ, tɯrme ra tu-ŋke-nɯ tɕe iɕqha nɯ, pjɯ-mto-nɯ tɕe iɕqha, 
kɯ-rɤpɕaʁ cho ju-kɯ-ɕe nɯnɯra kɯ pjɯ-mto-nɯ tɕe, nɤkinɯ, iɕqha nɯ, 
ɲɯ-sɤŋo-ŋɯ tɕe, kelaosi kɯ-xtɕi nɯ kɯ, nɯnɯ mbro kɯmŋu nɯ ntsɯ (ju-nɤm) /ju-no-nɯ/, (135)
tu-rɤma, nɯra tu-ɣɤɕqali nɯra pjɯ-mtshɤm-nɯ tɕe,

"wo a-mbro kɯmŋu ra, tɤ-stu-nɯ je, kɤ-rɤma tɤ-stu-nɯ" nɯra tu-ti tɕe rɤɣo chɯ-βze pjɤ-ŋu.
tɕendɤre kelaosi kɯ-wxti nɯnɯ kɯ tɕendɤre nɯra pjɤ-mtshɤm tɕe to-qha.
tɕendɤre nɯ ɯ-ŋgɯ zɯ, mbro kɯβde nɯ aʑɯɣ ɕti netɕi, kɯ-χsɤl ɕti ???ri" nɯra ɲɯ-sɯsɤm pjɤ-ŋu. (200)
tɕendɤre tɯ-rpa /to-joʁ tɕe/, nɤkinɯ, to-ndo tɕe, xiao kelaosi ɣɯ ɯ-mbro nɯ pjɤ-sat.
tɯ-rpa to-lɤt tɕe.
tɕendɤre kelaosi kɯ-xtɕi nɯnɯ, kɯ wuma ʑo pjɤ-nɯzdɯɣ tɕe tɕeri, 
ɯ-mbro nɯ ɣɯ, pɯ-kɤ-sat nɯ ɣɯ ɯ-ndʐi nɯ pjɤ-qaʁ.
tɕe /to/ to-sɯzbaʁ, to-sɯɣrom. (220)
tɕendɤre qale ɯ-ŋgɯ to-ɕkho tɕe, to-sɯɣrom tɕe, tɕendɤre tɤ-fkɯm ɯ-ŋgɯ chɤ-rku.
tɤ-fkɯm ɯ-ŋgɯ chɤ-rku tɕe, tɕendɤre kɤntɕhaʁ jo-tsɯm tɕe "z-ɲɯ-ntsɣe-a" /ɲɤ/ ɲɤ-sɯso tɕe jo-tsɯm.
tɕendɤre jɤ-ari ɯkhɯkha tɕe tɕendɤre, nɤkinɯ,
/tɯmɯ/ tɯrmɯ ko-ɣi tɕe ko-qanɯ.
tɕendɤre kelaosi kɯ-xtɕi nɯnɯ, kha nɤki, ŋkhorwapa ɣɯ, tɯrme ci ɣɯ, nɤkinɯ,
ɯ-kɯm nɯtɕu jo-zɣɯt.
tɕendɤre nɯ ɣɯ ɯ-khɯɣɲɟɯ nɯtɕu lo-nɯxsɯ ri, 
nɯnɯ, nɤki, nɯnɯ tɤ-mu nɯ kɯ, iɕqha nɯ, kɯ-lɤɣ rgɤtpu ci, nɯnɯtɕu,
maka ɯ-mgrɯn ra kɯ-saχaʁ cai cho to-βzu tɕe, pjɤ-k-ɤsɯ-mgrɯn tɕe pjɤ-nɤpɤri-ndʑi. (305)
tɕendɤre nɯ jamar tɕe tɕendɤre, kɯ-ɤrqhi tɕu ɲɯ-sɤŋo tɕe, nɤkinɯ, iɕqha,
mbro, kɯ mkhɯrlu ju-kɯ-rɤɕi nɯ ɣɯ ɯ-zgra ci pjɤ-mbri tɕe, 
tɕe iɕqha, tɤ-mu nɯ ɣɯ ɯ-nmaʁ nɯ ju-nɯɣi pjɤ-ŋu.
tɕendɤre, nɯnɯ tɤ-tɕɯ nɯ wuma ʑo ɯ-sɯm ra kɯ-sna ci pjɤ-ŋu tɕe, 
tɕeri wuma ʑo li ɯ-mɲɯtɕhɤz mɤ-kɯ-pe ci tɕe, iɕqha,  (330)

\c complement

nɤkinɯ iɕqha, tɤ-mthɯm thɯ-kɤ-pu, cha, nɯnɯra to-wum tɕe 
ndɤre cang ɯ-ŋgɯ lo-rku.
tɕendɤre iɕqha nɯ, li iɕqha, /rgɤt/ kɯlɤɣ rgɤtpu nɯnɯ rgɤm ɯ-ŋgɯ pjɤ-nɤtsɯ. (400 ko-sɤnbaʁ???)
tɯrme nɯ rgɤm ɯ-ŋgɯ pjɤ-rku tɕe pjɤ-nɤtsɯ.
iɕqha tɤ-nmaʁ nɯ jɤ-azɣɯt tɕe tɕendɤre,
kelaosi kɯ-xtɕi nɯ pjɤ-mto tɕe, 
"nɤʑo ɕɯ tɯ-ŋu, kutɕu tɕhindʐa ku-tɯ-rɤʑi, 
kha ɯ-pɕi zɯ ku-tɯ-rɤʑi tɕe, 
ɯ-ŋgɯ lɤ-ɣi, a-rca lɤ-ɣi" to-ti.
tɕendɤre kelaosi kɯ-xtɕi nɯ kɯ, nɯra pɯ-pɯ-kɯ-fse ʑo, ndɤre,
ɯʑo ɣɯ nɯɕɯŋgɯ pɯ-pɯ-kɯ-fse nɯnɯra, nɤkinɯ, pjɤ-fɕɤt.
tɕendɤre, nɤkinɯ, "aʑo nɯra fse-a tɕe ŋgɯ-a tɕe, 
kɤ-nɯɕe mɤ-cha-a tɕe, kutɕu ku-nɯrma-a ɯ́-jɤɣ?" to-ti.

nɯra tó-wɣ-nɤpɤri-ndʑi, /tɕip/ ndʑi-pɤri jo-ɣɯt.

nɯnɯ cang ɯ-ŋgɯ lajaʁ??? nɯra ɯ-ʁjitpa pjɤ-tu. (500)

tɕendɤre iɕqha nɯ, iɕqha ɯʑo ɯ-tɤ-fkɯm thɯ-kɤ-rku ɣɯ, maʁkɯ,
tɯ-ndʐi thɯ-kɤ-rku?? ɣɯ tɤ-fkɯm ɯ-taʁ nɯtɕu tɯ-qartsɯ ʁnɯz to-lɤt pjɤ-rɤtɕaʁ, (520)

\c relative.
tɕe kɯki tɤ-nmaʁ nɯ kɯ "nɯ ndɤre wuma ʑo ɲɯ-saχaʁ tɕe ɲɯ-pe" ɲɤ-sɯso tɕe tɕe, nɤkinɯ, (625)
nɤki, kelaosi kɯ-xtɕi nɯ ɯ-ɕki "aʑo kukutɕu, nɤkinɯ, nɤki, rŋɯl tʂaŋka tɯ-tɤfkɯm tu tɕe,
kɯki nɤʑo nɤ-tɤfkɯm ɯ-ŋgɯ sŋaʁspa nɯ chɯ-tɯ-nɯ-sɤmbi ɯ́-jɤɣ" to-ti ɲɯ-ŋu. (640)
tɕeri kelaosi kɯ-xtɕi nɯ nɯ ʁo lɯski tɕe wuma ʑo ɲɤ-rga tɕe /to/ "jɤɣ" to-ti, to-nɤla.
tɕe ɯ-fsosŋi tɕe tɕendɤre, nɤki, kelaosi kɯ-xtɕi nɯnɯ, ɯ-kha tɕu jo-nɯzɣɯt tɕe tɕendɤre, 
iɕqha kelaosi kɯ-wxti nɯ kɯ "nɯnɯra sthamtɕɤt rŋɯl tʂaŋka nɯ ŋotɕu jɤ-ɣe?" tu-ti. (705)
tɕeri kelaosi kɯ-xtɕi nɯ kɯ "aʑo, nɤki, a-mbro ɯ-ndʐi nɯ nɯ-ntsɣe-t-a tɕe, /nɯ kɯ/ nɯ ɣɯ ɯ-phɯ ŋu" to-ti.
