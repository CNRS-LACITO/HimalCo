tɕhɯɣar lo-sɯta.
tɕhɯɣar lo-sɯta tɕe tɕendɤre iɕqha nɯ, iɕqha tɯ-ci nɯnɯ, tɯ-tɣa a-tɤ-mbro tɕe, ɯʑo kɯ, iɕqha nɯ, tɕhɯɣar nɯnɯ li tɯ-tɣa tu-ɣɤmbrɤm, iɕqha nɯ, 
tɯ-ci nɯnɯ tɯ-rtsɤɣ ta-ɣɤmbro tɕe,
ɯʑo kɯnɤ li tɯ-rtsɤɣ tu-ɣɤmbrɤm pjɤ-ŋu tɕe tɕendɤre,
tɯ-ci, @bainiangzi kɯ tɯ-ci tɕhi kɯ-fse ta-nɯ-ɣɤwxti kɯnɤ, 
tɕe nɯ iɕqha rgɯnba nɯ, chɯ-sɯtsɯm mɯ-pjɤ-cha.
tɕendɤre @bainiangzi ɯtɤjɯ tɕe ɯ-skhrɯ mɯ-pjɤ-βdi tɕe, nɯnɯ sŋaʁspa nɯ kɤ-ɕɯnŋo mɯ-pjɤ-cha.
tɕe kɯ-maqhu tɕe tɕendɤre iɕqha, sŋaʁspa nɯ kɯ @bainiangzi nɯnɯ, nɯnɯ /kow/ kó-wɣ-ndo.
tɕendɤre χsɤr ɣɯ iɕqha, kontsi ɯ-ŋgɯ chɤ-sɯrku.
kontsi ɯ-ŋgɯ nɯtɕu pjɤ-rku tɕe tɕendɤre,
li fengta kɤti nɯnɯ, nɤki, mtɕhortɯn ɣɯ ɯ-qa zɯ pjɤ-sɯrku.
tɕe nɯtɕu pjɤ-sɯɲcɤr.
tɕendɤre xiaoqing nɯ kɯ ku-rtoʁ tɕe, ɯ-pi nɯnɯ, nɯnɯ mtɕhortɯn ɯ-pa nɯtɕu /pj/ pjɤ́-wɣ-sɯɲcɤr ɕti tɕe,
wuma ʑo, nɤkinɯ, kɯjŋu ʑo to-joʁ tɕe "nɯ a-pi chɯ-tɕat-a ra" ɲɤ-sɯso tɕe,
"tu-fsraŋ-a ra" ɲɤ-sɯso tɕe tɕendɤre,
zgo ɯ-ŋgɯ nɯtɕu ko-ʑɣɤnɤtsɯ tɕe, nɤkinɯ, ko-rɤntshom.
tɕendɤre iɕqha, /ɲɤʑɣɤs/ ɯ-βri ra ɲɤ-sɯxɕɤt,
"kɯ-maqhu tɕe tɕendɤre iɕqha, sŋaʁspa nɯ pjɯ-ɕɯnŋam-a ra" ɲɤ-sɯso.
tɕendɤre nɯra /pa/ nɤkinɯ, ka-rɤntshom ka-sthɯt tɕe tɕendɤre, nɤkinɯ to-stu iɕqha nɯ, 
chɤ-ɬoʁ tɕe tɕendɤre iɕqha, 
tɕetu, sŋaʁspa tɤ-wɯ nɯnɯ, nɤki iɕqha, qajɯ, tɕhɯʁjɯ, @pangxie kɤ-ti ɣɯ ɯ-xtu ɯ-ŋgɯ nɯtɕu chɤ-rku.
tɕendɤre ɯ-pi, nɯnɯ kɯ to-fsraŋ.
tɕe nɯ ɯ-qhu pɕintɕɤt tɕe, tɕendɤre @bainiangzi cho @xuxian ni li tɯtɯrca ku-nɯ-rɤʑi-ndʑi tɕe
wuma ʑo pjɤ-scit-ndʑi tu-ti ɲɯ-ŋu.
tɕe nɯ maka mɯ-pjɤ-nɯ-ɴɢɤt-ndʑi tu-ti ɲɯ-ŋu.
