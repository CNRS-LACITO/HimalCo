kɯɕɯŋgɯ tɕe dongguo xiansheng kɤ-ti ci pjɤ-tu.
dongguo xiansheng nɯnɯ, xiansheng nɯnɯ kɯɕɯŋgɯ tɕe sloχpɯn kɤ-ti pjɤ-ŋu.
tɕe nɯnɯ sloχpɯn tsa pjɤ-ŋu,
jɯɣi ntsɯ chɯ-nɤmɤle nɯ ntsɯ tu-rtoʁ pjɤ-ŋu.
tɕendɤre qapar ci, spjaŋkɯ ci pjɤ-tu, ɲɯ-ŋu ɣe?
tɕendɤre kɯɕɯŋgɯ tɕe nɯnɯ, jɯɣi ɯ-kɯ-rtoʁ nɯnɯ, 
dongguo xiansheng pjɤ-rmi tɕe,
ɯʑo jɯɣi ntsɯ tu-rtoʁ tɕe, ɯ-ŋgɯ nɯtɕu tɯrme kɯ-kɯ-pe nɯra pjɯ-rtoʁ pjɤ-ɕti ri, (030)
tɕeri tɯrme ra nɯ-ɕki tɕe, nɤkinɯ, iɕqha nɯ,
tɯrme ra nɯ-ɕki tɕe, wuma ʑo nɤki, li ɯ-pɤlɤjlɯz kɯ-me ci, ɯ-βlu ra kɯ-me ci pjɤ-ŋu. (040)
tɕe tɯ-sŋi tɕe iɕqha dongguo xiansheng nɯ kɯ, nɤkinɯ, pɯwɯ ci /tokɤ/ to-nɯmbrɤpɯ tɕe iɕqha,
kɯmaʁ rɟɤlkhɤβ tɕu ju-ɕe pjɤ-ŋu.
tɕe nɯ ju-ɕe pjɤ-ŋu tɕe, tɕendɤre, 
pɯwɯ ɯ-taʁ nɯtɕu iɕqha nɯ, li jɯɣi, kɯ-dɯ-dɤn, tɯ-tɤ-fkɯm kɯ-wxti ʑo pjɤ-k-ɤɣɯɕkat-ci.
tɕendɤre kɤ-ŋke wuma ʑo pjɤ-ɣɤβlo.
tɕendɤre iɕqha dongguo xiansheng nɯ kɯ ku-rtoʁ tɕe,
pɯwɯ kɯ-xtɕɯ-xtɕi ci pjɤ-ɕti,
li ɯ-taʁ jɯɣi kɯ-dɯ-dɤn tu-oɣɤɕkat tɕendɤre,  (110)
ɲɤ-znɯzdɯxpa, ɲɤ-znɯzdɯxpa qhendɤre, 
tɕendɤre pɯwɯ ɯ-taʁ nɯtɕu pjɤ-ɬoʁ qhe,
ɯʑo kɯ pɯwɯ nɯ tu-mtshi tɕe tu-ŋke pjɤ-ŋu.
tɕendɤre kɯ-dɤn sthɯci jo-ɕe mɯ-pjɤ-tsu ma tɕendɤre, (130 kɤrqhi?)
iɕqha pɯwɯ nɯ ko-rɤʑi.
tɕe nɯ sɤznɤ ɲɯ-ɣɤwu ntsɯ pjɤ-ŋu.
tɕendɤre, nɤkinɯ iɕqha nɯ, dongguo xiansheng nɯ, 
tɕe nɤki pɯwɯ nɯ kɯ nɯ pjɤ-mto ɕi pjɤ-mtshɤm kɯ ma,
spjaŋkɯ nɯ jo-ɣi ndʐa pjɤ-ŋu ma,
tɕendɤre nɤki, spjaŋkɯ ci jo-ɣi tɕe,
ndʑi-ɕki jo-ɣi.
tɕendɤre jɤ-ɣe tɕe tɕe, iɕqha dongguo xiansheng ɯ-ɕki nɯtɕe ɯ-χpɯm pjɤ-tshoʁ.
tɕendɤre "wortɕhi wojɤr ʑo, nɤkinɯ, 
tu-kɯ-qur-a ɯ́-jɤɣ, nɤʑo sloχpɯn wuma nɤ-sɯm kɯ-sna tɯ-ɕti tɕe, tu-kɯ-βri-a, tu-kɯ-fsraŋ-a ɯ́-jɤɣ ma,
kɯ-ɣɤrʁaʁ ra jɤ-ɣe-nɯ tɕe, ɣɯ-sat-a-nɯ ɲɯ-ŋu" to-ti.
tɕendɤre dongguo xiansheng nɯ kɯ ci chɤ-rɯsɯso ri,
"ŋu ri, nɤʑo spjaŋkɯ ɲɯ-tɯ-ɕti tɕetha kɯ-ndza-a ɕti" to-ti.
tɕeri iɕqha spjaŋkɯ nɯ kɯ ɯ-qom ra pjɤ-tɕɤt tɕe,
"aʑo spjaŋkɯ kɯ-pe ci ɕti-a tɕe mɤ-ta-ndza" ra to-ti.
tɕendɤre dongguo xiansheng nɯnɯ, li nɤkinɯ, mɯ-ɲɤ-znɤɕqa ɲɤ-znɯzdɯxpa qhe,
tɕendɤre nɤki, tɕe "ɲɯ-ŋu rɯtɕi, nɤʑo kɤ-fsraŋ tɕhi tu-ste-a?" to-ti;
tɕendɤre iɕqha nɯ, jo-ru nɤkinɯ,
dongguo xiansheng jo-ru tɕe iɕqha,
tɤ-fkɯm ɯ-ŋgɯ jɯɣi thɯ-kɤ-rku nɯ pjɤ-mto tɕe, 
"qhe mɯ-mɤ-ɲɯ-pe nɤ,  (256)
nɤʑo kɯki, tɤ-fkɯm ɯ-ŋgɯ nɯtɕu thɯ-ɕe ɯ-mɤ-kɯ-ntshi-ci" to-ti (300)
tɕendɤre "tɤ-fkɯm ɯ-ŋgɯ tɕe thɯ-ɕe ɲɯ-ntshi wo" to-ti.
tɕendɤre dongguo xiansheng nɯ kɯ iɕqha tɤ-fkɯm ɯ-ŋgɯ jɯɣi nɯra tsuku lo-tɕɤt,
tsuku lo-tɕɤt tɕe tɕendɤre, tɕendɤre nɤkinɯ iɕqha,
spjaŋkɯ nɯ nɯ ɯ-ŋgɯ chɤ-rku.
tɕe iɕqha nɯ, chɤ-rku qhe tɕendɤre,
ɯʑo kɯ li jɯɣi to-ndo qhe, nɯtɕu pjɤ-k-ɤ<nɯ>mdzɯ-ci qhe li jɯɣi to-rtoʁ.
tɕendɤre koŋla ʑo jɯɣi ra pjɤ-k-ɤsɯ-rtoʁ-ci.
tɕe toʁde qhendɤre, kɯ-ɣɤrʁaʁ ra jo-zɣɯt-nɯ.
kɯ-ɣɤrʁaʁ ra jo-zɣɯt-nɯ tɕe tɕendɤre nɤkinɯ,
dongguo xiansheng ɯ-ɕki to-thu-nɯ tɕe,
"iɕqha nɯtɕu, nɤki, kukutɕu, nɤki, spjaŋkɯ ci jɤ-kɯ-ɣe ɯ́-tu?" nɯra to-ti-nɯ.
tɕeri dongguo xiansheng nɯ kɯ, maka sthɯci, 
kɤ-ti mɤ-kɯ-spa ʑo pjɤ-fse tɕe ma daltsɯtsa ʑo "aʑo kɯkɯre ri jɯɣi pɯ-asɯ-rtoʁ-a ɕti tɕe,
maka pɯ-mto-t-a maka me." to-ti.
tɕendɤre nɤkinɯ, iɕqha kɯ-ɣɤrʁaʁ ra kɯ li ɲɤ-nɤstu-nɯ qhe, nɤki,
mɤ-kɤ-nɤstu ra pjɤ-me qhe, (410)
"khramba βze" mɯ-ɲɤ-sɯso-nɯ qhe, 
tɕendɤre li iɕqha spjaŋkɯ ɯ-kɯ-βɟi jo-ɕe-nɯ.
tɕendɤre nɤki iɕqha, kɯ-ɣɤrʁaʁ ra jɤ-arqhi-nɯ jamar tɕe tɕe,
dongguo xiansheng kɯ spjaŋkɯ ɯ-ɕki to-ti, 
"nɤʑo thɯ-ɬoʁ loβ, lɤ-ɬoʁ /loβ/ je ma,
kɯ-ɣɤrʁaʁ ra jɤ-ari-nɯ, kɯ-ɤrqhi tsa jɤ-arqhi-nɯ ɕti tɕe, (430)
thɯ-ɬoʁ je" to-ti.
tɕendɤre spjaŋkɯ nɯnɯ tɤ-fkɯm ɯ-ŋgɯ nɯtɕu lo-ɬoʁ tɕe, 
ɯ-xtu ra ɲɤ-nɤmɯma.
tɕendɤre, "wo aʑo rca, sŋɯqiɯ ʑo jɤ-rɟɯɣ-a pɯ-ra tɕe, wuma ʑo...
nɯ a-tɯ-mtsɯr nɯ!" nɯra to-ti.
"tɕendɤre nɤʑo kɯ-pe tɤ-tɯ-nɤma-t tɕe,
rŋama thɯ-tɕɤt tɕe,
tu-ta-ndza tɕe nɤkinɯ, a-xtu a-tɤ-fka ɲɯ-ntshi" to-ti.
tɕendɤre, dongguo xiansheng nɯ wuma ʑo ɯ-mbrɯ to-ŋgɯ tɕe,
"ki qapar ki maka, ɯ-tʂɯn kɯ-nɯ-jmɯt, 
ɯ-tʂɯn, ɯ-βzɤŋɤnŋu wuma kɯ-me ci ɲɯ-ŋu tɕe, (510)
ɲɤ-sɯso tɕe, wuma ɲɤ-nɯrɤŋom tɕeri, nɤkinɯ,
li spjaŋkɯ nɯ kɯ, dongguo xiansheng ɯ-ɕki "nɯnɯ kɯ-pe tɤ-tɯ-nɤma-t qhe rŋama thɯ-tɕɤt" to-ti.
tɕendɤre "chɯ-ta-ndza ra" /to/ to-ti.
tɕendɤre khro ʑo pjɤ-nɤndɯt-ndʑi ri,
tɕe nɤkinɯ, maka, /qapar/ spjaŋkɯ nm mɯ-pjɤ-khɯ qhendɤre,
kɯki iɕqha, sarsi, si ɣɯ ɯ-pa chiz jo-ɕe-ndʑi.
tɕendɤre dongguo xiansheng kɯ sarsi nɯ ɯ-ɕki to-ti tɕe,
"wo sarsi, nɤʑo thɯ-kɯ-rgɤz tɯ-ɕti tɕe, (545)
aʑo, nɤki, kɯki spjaŋkɯ ɣɯ ɯ-sroʁ kɤ-ri-t-a ri,
tham tɕe ɣɯ-ndza-a ɲɯ-ŋu tɕe,
nɯnɯ tu-fse? nɤ /ɯβrɤkɯ/, nɯnɯ ɯβrɤ-kɯ-pe-ci, (550)
nɯnɯ mɯ́j-pe, /tɕhindʐa/ mɤ-pe nɤ" nmra to-ti. "ɯβrɤ-kɯ-pe-ci" to-ti.
tɕendɤre ri iɕqha sarsi nɯ kɯ ɯ-ku ra pjɤ-phɤβ tɕe,
"aʑo pakuku ʑo a-mat ku-tshoʁ-a, kɯ-dɯ-dɤn ʑo ku-tshoʁ-a ɕti ri,
nɯra, nɤki, tɯrme ra kɯ chɯ-ndza-nɯ ʁɟa ʑo ɕti tɕe,
nɯnɯ tɕe li, nɯ ɲɯ-fse nɯ ɲɯ-raŋ kɤ-ti mɤ-kɯ-khɯ ci ɲɯ-ɕti tɕe, (615)
/tu/ tu-tɯ́-wɣ-ndza ɯ-ɲɯ-sɯsɤm qhe a-tɤ-tɯ́-wɣ-nɯ-ndza ɲɯ-ntshi" ra to-ti.
tɕendɤre nɤkinɯ, qapar kɯ (maʁ), 
spjaŋkɯ kɯ nɯ pa-mtshɤm tɕendɤre, ɲɤ-rga tɕe wuma ʑo to-ʑɣɤpa tɕe,
"ɯ-pɯ-tɯ-mtshɤm, nɤki, sarsi si nɯ kɯnɤ, nɤkinɯ, "aʑo kɯ a-tɤ-tɯ́-wɣ-ndza" tu-ti ɲɯ-ɕti" to-ti tɕe tɕendɤre
\c hybrid
nɯɕɯmɯma dongguo xiansheng ɯ-ɕki tɕe ko-mtsaʁ, iɕqha spjaŋkɯ nɯ.
tɕeri dongguo xiansheng kɯ li to-ti tɕe,
"wo, nɯ mɤ-pe, mɤʑɯ aʑo tɯtʂaŋ ɯ-kɯ-lɤt ci ɕar-a ntshi ma, nɯnɯ mɤ-pe" to-ti. (640)
tɕendɤre, nɤkinɯ, mbala ci pjɤ-tu tɕe,
nɯ ɯ-rkɯ jo-ɣi-ndʑi tɕe tɕe, 
dongguo xiansheng kɯ mbala ɯ-ɕki to-ti tɕe
"mbala-do, aʑo, nɤkinɯ, spjaŋkɯ ki ɣɯ ɯ-sroʁ kɤ-ri-t-a ŋu ri,
tham tɕe, aʑo ɣɯ-ndza-a ɲɯ-ŋu tɕe, 
nɤʑo tɤ-ti tɕe, ki tu-fse nɯ, ɯβrɤ-kɯ-pe-ci?" to-ti. (710)
