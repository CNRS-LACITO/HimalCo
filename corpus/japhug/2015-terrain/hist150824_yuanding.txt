nɯ ɣɯ ɯ-mɯntoʁ kɯ-ji ci pjɤ-tu. (10 possessif)
tɕe kha nɯtɕu ɯ-rkoz ʑo ɯ-ʁjoʁ pjɤ-ŋu wo, ɲɤ-ɕar tɕe, 
nɯ kɯ ɯ-cai cho ɯ-mɯntoʁ ntsɯ pjɯ-ji pjɤ-ŋu.
tɕendɤre nɯnɯ iɕqha nɯ, kɯki tɕe iɕqha, kɤntɕhaʁ ɯ-rkɯ kɯ-ɤrmbat tsa chiz kha ci pjɤ-tu tɕe,
nɯnɯ tɕe, nɤkinɯ, si wu li jamar ma pjɤ-me.
nɤkinɯ, tɕe nɯnɯ tɕu kha ɯ-mbe tsa ci pjɤ-tu.
tɕe kha ɯ-mbe nɯ ɣɯ, iɕqha nɯ, ɯ-znde nɯ wuma ʑo pjɤ-jaʁ.
wuma ʑo kɯ-jaʁ ci pjɤ-ŋu, tɕe mɤʑɯ kha ɣɯ ɯ-taʁ mtɕhortɯn kɯ-fse ra pjɤ-tu.
tɕe nɯ-kha ɣɯ ɯ-ku nɯ ku-ɤmtɕɯmtɕoʁ ʑo tɕe, (100)
zgo tsa kɯ-fse pjɤ-ŋu.
tɕendɤre tɯ-xpa tɕe ftɕar jo-zɣɯt tɕe, iɕqha nɤkinɯ, βdaχpu nɯra kɯ-mɤɕi ci pjɤ-ŋu tɕe, 
tɕe nɯnɯtɕu, iɕqha nɯ, nɯtɕu kɯ-rɤrma jo-ɣi-nɯ.
tɕe nɯnɯ kɯ-rɤrma jo-ɣi-nɯ tɕe, nɤki, nɯ-laχtɕha, nɤkinɯ,
nɯ-laχtɕha, kha ɣɯ nɯ-rɟɯ laχtɕha ɯ-ŋgɯz nɯtɕu,
nɯ-kha wuma ʑo kɯ-mpɕɤr ci pjɤ-tu.
tɕe kha ɣɯ ɯ-ʁɤri nɯtɕu, nɤkinɯ, wuma ʑo, kɤ-pe ci, kɯ-sɤscit ci, nɤkinɯ, stɤmku pjɤ-tu. (137 kɯpe?)
