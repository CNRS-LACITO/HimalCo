\c relative (temps)
@zhuyingtai nɯnɯ, nɤki iɕqha, jiaozi ɯ-taʁ nɯtɕu, nɤkinɯ, /tɤ/ tɤ́-wɣ-nɯthaj tɕe /joton/ tɕe jó-wɣ-tsɯm.
