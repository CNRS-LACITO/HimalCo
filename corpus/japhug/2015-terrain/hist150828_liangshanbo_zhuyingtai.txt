zhu yingtai kɤ-ti ci pjɤtu.
tɕendɤre kɯɕɯŋgɯ tɕe iɕqha, zhu jia ɣɯ nɯ-sɤtɕha nɯnɯ, nɯ-tshupa nɯtɕu 
zhu jia nɯtɕu, dizhu ci pjɤ-tu.
ɯ-xing nɯ zhu pjɤ-ŋu.
tɕe zhu pjɤ-ŋu tɕe tɕendɤre,
ɯ-rmi nɯ zhu yuanwai pjɤ-rmi.
tɕendɤre zhu yuanwai ɣɯ ɯ-me ci pjɤ-tu.
tɕe ɯ-rmi nɯ zhu yingtai pjɤ-rmi.
tɕe nɯ zhu yingtai nɯnɯ, nɤkinɯ iɕqha nɯ, wuma ʑo kɯ-ɕqraʁ kɯ-mpɕɤr ci pjɤ-ŋu.
tɕendɤre iɕqha, mɯntoʁ ra ɯ-tɯ-tʂɯβ, 
ɕoʁɕoʁ kɯ mɯntoʁ ra ɲɯ-sɯβze ɲɯ-qri ra pjɤ-spa.
tɕendɤre kɤ-rɤrɤt cho kɤ-rɤβzjoz nɯra, 
jɯɣi kɤ-ndɯn nɯra wuma ʑo pjɤ-rga.
tɕendɤre saβde-pɤrme thɯ-azɣɯt tɕendɤre,
zhu yingtai nɯ kɯ, nɤkinɯ, "tɤt-tɕɯ tu-nɯɕpɯz-a tɕe, nɤkinɯ, hangzhou nɯnɯtɕu kɯ-rɤβzjoz ju-ɕe-a ra" ɲɤ-sɯso.
tɕendɤre ɯ-mu ɯ-wa ni kɯ nɯsthi mɯ-pjɤ-nɤla-ndʑi tɕe tɕe nɯ "a-tɤ-nɤla-ndʑi ra" kɤ-sɯso kɯ, nɤʀinɯ,
tɤ-tɕɯ ɯ-ŋga ɕ-to-ŋga.
tɤ-tɕɯ ɯ-ŋga ɕ-to-ŋga qhendɤre, nɤkinɯ /ɯ-ŋgɯ loɕe/
jɯɣi ɯ-sɤ-ta kha nɯtɕu jo-ɕe,
tɕendɤre nɯtɕu pjɤ-k-ɤsɯ-rtoʁ-ci.
(ki ku-pe-a ma, ɲɤ-nɯjmɯt-a).
=====
tɕendɤre nɯtɕu tɤ-tɕɯ ɯ-ŋga to-ŋga qhe, nɯtɕu jɯɣi pjɤ-k-ɤsɯ-rtoʁ-ci.
tɕe ɯ-jaʁ nɯtɕu jɯɣi kɯ-jɯ-jaʁ ci ʑo pjɤ-k-ɤsɯ-ndo-ci tɕe rcanɯ,
/maka/ koŋla ʑo, nɤki, ɯ-sɯm kɤ-χtɤt ʑo /pjɤ/ pjɤ-k-ɤsɯ-ndɯn-ci. (200)
tɕe toʁde tɕendɤre iɕqha nɯ, zhu yuanwai chonɤ ʁzɤmi lo-ɣi-ndʑi, 
kha ɯ-ŋgɯ nɯtɕu lo-ɣi-ndʑi tɕe, 
tɕendɤre nɯtɕu tɕe tɤtɕɯpɯ ndʑi-mɤ-kɤ-nɯfse ci pjɤ-rɤβzjoz,
nɯtɕu jɯɣi pjɤ-k-ɤsɯ-ndɯn-ci 
tɕendɤre lo-ɕe-ndʑi tɕe, 
"nɤʑo ɕɯ ra nɯ-tɕɯ, ɕɯ ra nɯ-rɟit tɯ-ŋu?" to-ti-ndʑi.
tɕeri zhu yingtai kɯ, ku-rtoʁ tɕe tɕendɤre, "a-mu a-wa ni kɤ-nɯβlu pjɤ-cha-a" ɲɤ-sɯso rcanɯ, wuma ʑo ɲɤ-rga tɕe,
sɤz-nɤrɯre ʑo kɯ ɯ-rte ra pjɤ-mɟa.
tɕendɤre ɯ-kɤrme ra to-nɯɬoʁ tɕe,
"a-mu, a-wa, aʑo ɕti-a" nɯra to-ti.
tɕe nɯ zhu yuanwai chondɤre ɯ-rʑaβ ni, kɯ nɤki, pa-mto-ndʑi tɕe,
ɲɯ-nɤre-ndʑi ri pjɤ-maʁ, ɲɯ-ɣɤwu-ndʑi ri kɯ-maʁ kɯ-fse ɲɤ-k-ɤβzu-ci.
tɕendɤre zhu yingtai kɯ ɯ-wa ɯ-jaʁ ko-ndo tɕe,
to-znɤlɯli ʑo to-ti tɕe, "a-wa aʑo hangzhou tɕe kɯ-rɤβzjoz ɲɯ-ɕe-a tɕe, /ɯ-ɲɯ-pe?/" to-ti./ ɯ́-jɤɣ" nɯra to-ti.
tɕeri zhu yuanwai ɯ-ku ɲɤ-sɤrlɤrlɤɣ,
ki to-stu ɯ-ku ɲɤ-sɤrlɤrlɤɣ tɕe, nɤki,
"ŋotɕu zɯ, tɕheme ɯ-pɕi kɯ-ɕe, ɕɯ-kɯ-rɤβzjoz pɯ-tɯ-sɯz?" to-ti ɲɯ-ŋu.
