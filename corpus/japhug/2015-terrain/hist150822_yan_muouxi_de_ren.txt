\c ablatif 
/to/ nɤkinɯ iɕqha, muou nɯra to-nɯrdoʁ tɕe, ɯ-rgɤm ɯ-ŋgɯ nɯtɕu pjɤ-rku.

tɕendɤre ɯ-fkaβ pa-ta tɕe tɕe ɯ-taʁ nɯtɕu ko-k-ɤmdzɯ-ci.
ɯ-rgɤm nɯ ɯ-ŋgɯ nɯtɕu iɕqha, muou nɯra pjɤ-rku tɕe, to-nɯrdoʁ tɕe pjɤ-rku.

tɕe iɕqha ɯ-jmŋo pa-βzu tɕe wuma ʑo pɯ-kɯ-ɤtshɤxtshɯ nɯra ɲɤ-me tɕe tɕe ɯ-sɯm nɯ ɲɤ-ɴɢu kɯ-fse.

\c benefactive
