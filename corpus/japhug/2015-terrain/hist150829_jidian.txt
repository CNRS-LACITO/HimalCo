tɕendɤre rcanɯ, maka kɤ-rtsi mɤ-kɯ-sɤcha ʑo, laʁsta, ndzrɯ, tɯrpa nɯra /ɲɤ/ /ɲɤ-sɤβzu/ pjɤ-tɕɤt. (1140)
tɕendɤre tɯrme nɯra kɯ, tɯrme ɲɯ-dɤn-nɯ ɕti tɕe, nɯra to-ndo-nɯ tɕe,
tɯ-sŋi tɯ-rʑaʁ qhe tɕendɤre, @luohan nɯnɯ, skɯ nɯnɯ, kɯmŋu-ri nɯ to-sɤβzu-nɯ. (1200)
to-sɤβzu-nɯ qhe nɯ ɯ-qhu qhe tɕe, @jidian nɯ, nɯtɕu zgoku tɕe kɯkɯra sthamtɕɤt to-βzu-nɯ, kɯki, 
nɯnɯ skɯ ʁɟa ɲɤ-sɤβzu-nɯ.
tɕendɤre nɤkinɯ, @jidian nɯ kɯ-nɤrɯra jo-ɕe ri, iɕqha tɯrme ra kɯ, iɕqha @luohan skɯ nɯ ta-βzu-nɯ tɕe, 
skɯ ɯʑo nɯ to-βzu-nɯ ri, ɯ-mɲaʁ mɯ-to-βzu-nɯ, ɯ-mɲaʁrme mɯ-to-βzu-nɯ. (1220 tɤmɲaʁrme?)
tɕendɤre nɤkinɯ, nɯ rdɤstaʁ ɣɯ ɯ-skɯ nɯnɯ, pɯ-kɯ-nɯʑɯβ ʑo pjɤ-fse matɕi ɯ-mɲaʁrme cho pjɤ-me.
tɕendɤre "nɯ a-pɯ-ŋu tɕe tɕe, nɤki, mɯ́j-pe" ɲɤ-sɯso qhe, tɕendɤre
@jidian /huofo/ nɯnɯ kɯ, @heshang nɯ kɯ, ɯ-ndzrɯ kɯ kɯki, nɤki, @luohan kɯmŋu-ri nɯra ɣɯ, ci ɯ-mphru ci ɣɯ nɯ-mɲaʁrme to-βzu, 
/ɯ-jaʁ/ ɯ-ndzrɯ ko-sɤtsa tɕe nɯ-mɲaʁ to-βzu.
tɕendɤre nɤkinɯ, nɯ-mɲaʁrme ko-rɤt tɕendɤre iɕqha nɯ, 
ɯ-ndzrɯ ko-sɤtsa qhe nɯ-mɲaʁ to-βzu.
tɕendɤre kɯki ra, nɤki, @luohan kɯra ɣɯ nɯ-mɲaʁ chondɤre nɯ-mɲaʁrme to-tu tɕe, tɕendɤre,
kɯ-sɯsu ʑo to-fse-nɯ tɕe, wuma ʑo pjɤ-pe.
tɕe nɯ-ʁzɯɣ ra kɯ-pe kɯ-fse ɲɤ-sɤβzu.
tɕe nɯ pjɤ-fse tɕe tɕe, kɯki zgo ki, nɯ ɯ-qhu tɕe, 
tɕe kɯki, nɤki, skɯ kɯmŋu-ri nɯ kɯ ku-ɣɯrndi tɕe, (1325)
tɕe nɯ ɯ-qhu tɕe tɕendɤre, kɯki @lingyinsi ɯ-ʁɤri nɯtɕu pjɤ-rɤʑi tɕe, tɕe ju-nɤɕɯɕe mɯ-ɲɤ-cha.
tɕe mɯ-ɲɤ-cha tɕe tɯrme ra kɯ ɯ-rmi to-tɕɤt-nɯ tɕe, 
"feilaifeng" tɕe "zgo kɤ-nɯqambɯmbjom jɤ-kɯ-ɣe" to-sɤrmi-nɯ.
