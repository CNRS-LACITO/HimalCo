mɤʑɯ tɕhi tu?
=====================
zwɤr kɤ-ti ci tu tɕe,
zwɤr ɯ-tɯphu antɕhɯ.
ma zwɤrqha kɤ-ti ci tu tɕe,
zwɤrqha nɯnɯ si tsa fse.
tɕe ɯ-ru nɯra si ɯ-ru fse tɕe nɯnɯ,
ɯ-di wuma ʑo sɤjloʁ ma nɯnɯ
tɯji ɯ-mŋu ɯ-ndo ra zwɤrqha tu-ti-nɯ tɕe,
si wuma tu-mbro mɤ-kɯ-cha ci ŋu tɕe,
nɯnɯ zwɤrqha rmi.
tɕe nɯ kɯmaʁ ci tɕe, nɤkinɯ,
zwɤɣrum kɤ-ti ci tu tɕe, ɯnɯnɯ sɯjno ŋu tɕeri 
li tɤ-wxti tɕe ɯ-ru nɯra chɯ-rko chɯ-rgɤz cha ri,
ɯ-jwaʁ nɯra ɯ-dɯχɯn kɯ-fse ci tu tɕe,
ɯ-di χɕɤβ.
ri zwɤrqha kɯ-fse ɯ-di mɤ-sɤjloʁ.
ma nɯnɯ nɯŋa ra kɯ tu-ndza-nɯ ŋgrɤl.
kú-wɣ-sqa tɕe, paʁ ra kɯnɤ tu-ndza-nɯ ŋgrɤl.
tɯrme kɤ-ndza mɤ-sna.
tɕendɤre, nɤki, zwɤrɲaʁ kɤ-ti ci tu tɕe,
nɯnɯ ɯ-mdoʁ nɯnɯ kɯ-qandʐi kɯ-fse, kɯ-ɤɲaʁndzɯm kɯ-fse ci ŋu tɕe,
nɯ zwɤr... zwɤrɲaʁ ŋu tɕe
nɯ fsapaʁ ra kɯ ndza-nɯ ri, wuma sthɯci mɤ-rga-nɯ.
tɕe ɯ-tshɯɣa nɯra zwɤɣrum cho naχtɕɯɣ ɕti.
tɯji ɯ-ŋgɯ tu-ɬoʁ tɕe wuma ʑo qha-nɯ ma
ʑaʑa ʑo tu-rko ŋu tɕe kɤ-phɯt ɴqa tɕe,
nɯnɯ, nɤkinɯ, sɯjno qha-nɯ tɕe, ɲɯ-kɤ-ɣɤme ftɕaka ntsɯ tu-βzu-nɯ ŋu.
tɕe nɯnɯ zwɤr... tɕe ɴqiazwɤr kɤ-ti ci tu.
ɴqiazwɤr kɤ-ti ci tu tɕe, nɯnɯ ɴqiaβ pɕoʁ tu-ɬoʁ ɲɯ-ŋu tɕe, núndʐa ɴqiazwɤr tu-ti-nɯ ŋu tɕe,
ɯnɯnɯ tɕe kɯ-nɯkɤrŋi ju-kɯ-ɕe tɕe li 
tu-tɯ-ɬoʁ ʑo nɯ kɯ-mpɯ-mpɯ nɯnɯ ɲɯ́-wɣ-phɯt tɕe 
tɕe li, nɤkinɯ, tú-wɣ-ɣle tɕe tú-wɣ-ndza sna.
tɕe @cai kɯ-fse tú-wɣ-sɯ-βzu khɯ.
jinde tɕe, nɯ sthɯci ɯ-kɯ-ndza maŋe ma
nɯ ɕɯŋgɯ tɕe, ɯ-kɯ-rga pɯ-dɤn.
tɕe nɯnɯ ɴqiazwɤr ŋu
tɕe zwɤr nɯ ɯ-tɯphu antɕhɯ ma
==================
mɤʑɯ ɕoʁɕɣa kɤ-ti ci tu ri, ɯ-jwaʁ nɯra li zwɤr kɯ-fse ŋu tɕe,
nɯnɯ li sɯjno ɕti tɕe,
tɯji ɯ-ŋgɯ tu-ɬoʁ ra qha-nɯ tɕe,
tɯji ɯ-ŋgɯ ɯ-ndo tɤrɤku ɯ-rca nɯra ntsɯ tu-ɬoʁ ŋu tɕe,
tɕhi kɤ-cha ʑo chɯ-phɯt-nɯ,
tɕe chɯ-βde-nɯ nɯ maʁ nɤ
fsapaʁ ɲɯ-mbi-nɯ ŋgrɤl ma
ɕoʁɕɣa rmi ri, li zwɤr tɤrca ŋu.
tɕe zwɤr ɯ-tɯphu antɕhɯ wo.
