qro kɤ-ti ci tu
qro nɯnɯ dɯdɯt cho ɯ-tɯ-wxti naχtɕɯɣ.
tɕeri qro nɯnɯ wuma ʑo wɣrum, kɯ-wɣrɯ-wɣrum ʑo ŋu.
ɯ-jme ɯ-ku ri kɯ-xtɕɯ-xtɕi ʑo ma nɯ mɯma ʑo nɯ kɯ-wɣrum ŋu. 
ɯ-jme nɯ ɯ-pa pɕoʁ ɲɯ-wɣrum ɕti, ɯ-qhu chu pɕoʁ nɯnɯreri, sɤ... tɯ-tɤkhrɤz kɯ-ɲaʁ ɣɤʑu. 
tɕe qro nɯnɯ li tɯtɯrca ʁɟa ʑo ku-rɤʑi-nɯ ɕti tɕe,
nɯnɯ dɤn-nɯ, ci ci tɕe tɯtɯrca kɤ-rɤʑi(-nɯ)
tɯ-kɯmŋɤ-sqi kɯ-fse tɯtɯrca tu-nɯ ŋgrɤl. 
tɕe lɤ-zo-nɯ kɯnɤ tɯtɯrca lu-zo-nɯ, thɯ-nɯqambɯmbjom-nɯ kɯnɤ tɯtɯrca chɯ-nɯqambɯmbjom-nɯ
\c (fronting)
tɕe nɯnɯ wuma ʑo qɯmdroŋ kɯ-fse kɯ-ɤʑɯrja kɯ-βdi kɯ-fse mɯ́j-nɯqambɯmbjom-nɯ ri,
tɯtɯrca ʁɟa ʑo ɲɯ-nɯqambɯmbjom-nɯ ɲɯ-ŋu tɕe,
tɯtɯrca kɯ-rɤʑi nɯ wuma ʑo dɤn ŋgrɤl 
ma qro nɯnɯ, nɤkinɯ, ɯ-mi nɯra ɲɯ-ɣɯrni.
ɯ-mi nɯra ɲɯ-ɣɯrni ɯ-ŋgɯ kɯ ɲɯ-qarŋe kɯ-fse
tɕe rɟaspa ɲɯ-mpɕɤr ma ɯnɯnɯ qro ŋu tɕe
ɯ-mɲaʁ nɯ ɯ-rkɯ nɯ ɲɯ-ɣɯrni.
dɯdɯt cho ndʑi-tɯ-wxti naχtɕɯɣ ʑo.
ma dɯdɯt nɯnɯ ʁnɯz χsɯm jamar ma mɯ́j-rɤʑi ma 
qro nɯ kɯ dɤn tsa ʑo tɯtɯrca ku-rɤʑi ŋgrɤl.
ma nɯnɯ qro ŋu tɕe, wɣrum tɕe,
qro nɯnɯ nɤkinɯ, qro ɣɯ ɯ-ɕa, ɯ-se nɯra
tɤte //s// pha ɯ-phoŋbu ʑo smɤn nɯ 
tɯ-ku kɯ-mŋɤm phɤn 
iɕqha tɯ-kɤrnoʁ kɯ-mtɕɯr kɯ-fse, tɯ-sɯso kɯ-me kɯ-fse,
ɯnɯnɯra phɤn tu-ti-nɯ ŋgrɤl
tɕe nɯnɯ pjɯ-sat-nɯ tɕe nɯnɯ tu-ndza-nɯ tɕe ɲɯ-ŋgrɤl.
ri kɤ-ntsɣe ɣɤʑu tɕe, nɯreri, smɤn ɯ-spa zhuanmen tu-χtɯ-nɯ tɕe,
tɤ-rtɕhi ɯ-spa tu-χtɯ-nɯ.
tɯ-ku ɣɯ ɯ-rtɕhi ɯ-spa nɯ kɯ-fse tu-χtɯ-nɯ ɲɯ-ŋgrɤl.
tɕe nɯnɯ qro ŋu tɕe, qro nɯnɯ kɯ-sɤjndɤt ci ɕti tɕe, nɤkinɯ,
ri tɤrɤku ra ndze nɤ,
tɤrɤku ra ndze, qajɯ ra ndze,
tɕeri sɯmat tu-ndze, kɯmɯxte nɯ qajɯ cho sɯmat tu-ndze ɲɯ-ɕti ma,
tɯ-ji ɯ-ŋgɯ nɯ ci ci ma ɣi mɤ-ŋgrɤl.
tɕe tɤrɤku nɯ kɤ́-wɣ-nɯ-phɯt ɯ-qhu ri tɕe, 
nɯnɯ tɤ-rtsho ɯ-ŋgɯ ri lu-zo-nɯ ŋgrɤl.

tɕe nɯ qro ŋu.

ma nɯnɯ qro ŋu
======
qusput kɤ-ti ci tu.
tɕe qusput kɤ-ti nɯnɯ li pɣa ci ŋu tɕe nɤkinɯ,
li dɯdɯt cho ndʑi-mdoʁ naχtɕɯɣ ʑo.
qusput kɯ ɯ-jme hanɯni ɲɯ-zri (qusput ɣɯ ɯ-jme kɯ hanɯni ɲɯ-zri)
\c  ergative
ma nɯnɯ qusput ŋu tɕe,
qusput nɯ ɯʑo pɯ-kɤ-sat ʑo kɤ-mto mɯ-pɯ-rɲo-t-a ri,
nɯnɯ kú-wɣ-rtoʁ tɕe, nɯ kɯ-ɤrqhi ju-kɯ-ru tɕe, nɤkinɯ,
qusput nɯnɯ dɯdɯt tsa ɲɯ-fse, ɯ-mdoʁ nɯ dɯdɯt ɲɯ-fse.
tɕeri, nɤkinɯ, iʑora ɣɯ kɯkɯtɕu wuyue shiyihao jamar ri tɕe ju-ɣi tɕe tu-mbri ŋu tɕe,
ju-ru-nɯ tɕe, tɤ-mbri tɕe ci ci tɕe "qusput" tu-ti tɕe 
ɯ-jme kɯki kɯ-ɤntɤm ki kɯ-fse kundi ki tu-ste tɕe, 
ci ɣɤʑu, ci tu-mbri tɕe "qusput qusput" tu-ti ɲɯ-ŋu tɕe, 
tɕe nɯnɯ tu-ti ŋgrɤl tɕe,
kɯ-wxti ra kɯ nɯnɯ mixin ɣɯ ɯ-skɤt ɲɯ-ɕti ma, nɤkinɯ,
ki kɯ-fse kundi ku-ɕe nɯnɯ tɕe taχpa mɤ-pe ma 
iɕqha χtsiɯ nɯ ɣɯ ɯ-tsa ma tɯjpu me tɕe núndʐa ŋu tɕe 
\c （刚刚把升的口填满）
ki tu-ste tɕe ki ta-stu tɕe taχpa khro mɤ-pe,
tɤ-mbri tɕe "qusput" ta-tɯt tɕe 
ki tu-fse, taʁki tu-βze tɕe nɯnɯ χtsiɯ nɯ pjɯ-ɣnde ŋu tɕe
pjɯ-ɣnde kɤ-ti ɲɯ-ŋu tɕe nɯnɯ tɕe
nɯ a-tɤ-ste taχpa pe ra tu-ti-nɯ ŋgrɤl ma
nɯnɯ qusput ŋu.
tɕe tɤ-mbri tɕe ɯ-skɤt mpɕɤr ma "qusput qusput" ntsɯ tu-ti ŋu.
tɕe nɯnɯ qusput ŋu tɕe nɯnɯ
tɕeri ʑatsa ri tɯ-sla jamar tu-mbri qhe tɕe ɲɯ-me ɕti.
tɕe ŋotɕu ju-ɕe ŋu mɤxsi ma
tɕe nɯ ɯ-kɯ-ti ɲɯ-me ŋu 
\c （他叫声消失了）
tɕe wuyue shiyihao tɕe liuyue shiyihao jamar,
liuyue kɤ-ndzoʁ jamar ri tɕe tɕe kɤ-mtshɤm ɲɯ-me ɕti
tɕe nɯ qusput ŋu.

tɕe koŋla rmɤβja nɯ ʑo fse ŋu ɕi kɯma,
ɯ-jme nɯra koŋla kongque nɯ kɯsthɯci nɯ mɯ́j-zri cho

tɕe kɯki ɯ-xtɤpa kɯra kɯnɤ kɯ-qarŋe kɯ-wɣrum kɯ-fse chɯ-ɕe ɲɯ-ŋu.
ma nɯnɯ sɯŋgɯ rmɤβja kɤ-ti nɯ tu-mbri kɯ-fse kɤ-mtshɤm mɯ-pɯ-rɲo-t-a.
tɕe tɯ-ɣjɤn ko-ndo-nɯ tɕendɤre pɯ-mto-t-a ri, nɤkinɯ
ɯ-muj nɯra wuma ʑo ɲɯ-mpɕɤr.
tɕe qajɯ kɯ-fse tu-ndze ɲɯ-ŋu ma nɯ ma tɤrɤku mɯ́j-ndze.

chɯ-wɯwum kɯ-fse ra ŋgrɤl mɤ-ŋgrɤl mɤxsi
