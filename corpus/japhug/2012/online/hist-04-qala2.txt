qala nɯnɯ pɯ-pɯ-ŋu nɯ, tɕe nɯnɯ ra fse tɕe,
nɤkinɯ, ri qala nɯnɯ ɯ-zda ɯ-taʁ kɯ-ŋɤn maka mɤ-cha
tɕe ɯ-kɯ-ndza nɯ rcanɯ, ɯ-ʁgra xcat ʑo ɕti ma
nɤki, nɤmkha zɯ qajdo, qaliaʁ nɯra kɯ ri tú-wɣ-ndza ɕti 
ɯ-thoʁ zɯ khɯna lɯlu tɕendɤre qapar spjaŋkɯ kɯrtsɤɣ nɯ ra ɣɯ
nɯ-jaʁ nɯ-ɣi qhe li tú-wɣ-ndza ɕti 
tɕe ɯʑo ɯ-sɤ-ɤmɯmi nɯ dɤn ma ca kɯ-fse qaʑo kɯ-fse, tshɤt kɯ-fse
ɯʑo cho kɯ-naχtɕɯɣ sɯjno, xɕaj ma mɤ-kɯ-ndza nɯra chonɤ amɯmi-nɯ tɕe, 
qala kɯ tɤ-mthɯm mɤ-ndze tɕe, xɕaj ʁɟa ʑo tu-ndze ɕti 
tɕe mɤʑɯ iɕqha si ɣɯ ɯ-mat nɯra pɯ-kɯ-ŋgra nɯra tu-ndze ma nɯ ma ndze me tɕe nɯ,
qala nɯnɯ ɯʑo pe ma ɯ-sɤ-ŋɤn nɯ me ri, ɯ-kɯ-nɯʁgra nɯ dɤn
tɕe nɯ kɯ-fse ŋu.
